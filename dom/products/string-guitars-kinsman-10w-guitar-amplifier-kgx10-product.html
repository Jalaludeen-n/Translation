Kinsman 10W Guitar Amplifier KGX10

This lightweight but durable practice amp is perfect for budding guitarists. Its small size makes it perfect for practice sessions, and the perfect choice for musicians with limited space at home.

It has input for one instrument and headphones jack, so you can also practise silently.

Small but powerful, the Kinsman KGX10 delivers 10 watts, with separate controls for treble, mid and bass; master volume and gain, with push button channel & drive select, with a headphone jack. 

This compact amp with its simple, easy to use layout is great for beginners.

Features of the Kinsman 10W Guitar Amplifier KGX10:

  • 10 watts
  • 1/4" instrument input jack socket
  • Volume & gain controls
  • 2 Channels: Clean & Overdrive
  • Treble, middle and bass controls
  • Headphone socket
  • Grained vinyl covering
  • Vintage pattern speaker cloth
  • Carry handle
  • Rubber feet
  • Recessed, front-loaded control panel

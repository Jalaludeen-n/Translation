Beschreibung

 
Irish Songs for Ukulele by Dick Sheridan 

The ukulele is the perfect lightweight, compact, portable instrument - ideal for travel! It's also a wonderfully accessible instrument for musicians of any age or ability.
 
Ukulele Irish music isn't something you hear often, but whether you're an experienced strummer or just starting out, if you want to try your hand at learning Irish music, this is the ukulele tutor for you. 
 
Dick Sheridan's Irish Songs for Ukulele contains a collection of 55 Irish favourites. Each song is specifically arranged for the ukulele, with the melody provided in both standard notation and easy to read tab.
 
Featuring favourites such as Cockles and Mussels, Danny Boy, The Foggy Dew, The Irish Rover, Spancil Hill, The Rose of Tralee, Whiskey in the Jar and many more, this is the perfect book for anyone looking to brush up on their ballads and learn Irish music on the Ukulele.
 
'This book was full of great traditional Irish tunes. What was best about it was the fact it had tabs, chords, notes and lyrics.'

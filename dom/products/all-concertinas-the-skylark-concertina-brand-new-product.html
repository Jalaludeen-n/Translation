Beschreibung

 The Brand New Skylark Concertina

“You’ll notice the difference immediately, without overspending”

The Skylark is one of the most advanced concertinas in its class, built for serious players who want the speed and clarity of a premium instrument, with the kind of tone and touch you'd expect from a box twice the price.

This 30-button Anglo concertina in C/G features Czech-made ‘tipo a mano’ reeds for a clear, lively tone that cuts through even the busiest pub sessions without overpowering. The action is quick. The buttons are smooth. And every triplet, cut, and roll feels clean and crisp under your fingers.

If you’ve outgrown your Wren, your Swan, or any starter box, and you're ready to sound like the player you’ve worked to become, the Skylark was built for you.

Product highlights

Tone, Control and Confidence to Hold Your Own at Any Session

Some players believe the only next step is to go antique or boutique. But are they worth the cost, the wait, or the hassle? The Skylark was built for serious playing.

Fast action, clean tone, and reeds that respond the second you touch them. No vintage quirks, no waiting lists, no wondering if it sounds good or been set up right. This is a serious box for serious players that's ready for the session, right out of the case.

Fast, Crisp Response

The Skylark’s hand-finished ‘tipo a mano’ reeds are known for their bright, punchy tone and quick response, perfect for fast-paced reels and ornament-heavy playing. These premium reeds make every note and ornamentation ring out clearly, even in noisy sessions.

Effortless Speed and Control

With Riveted Action, the Skylark’s domed buttons are tight and responsive, giving you that springy, clean feel serious players demand. No sluggishness or sticking, just precise, effortless action that makes fast tunes feel fluid and fun.

It’s not just about power, though. The Skylark’s 7-fold leather bellows offer excellent air control, so you can phrase with precision and hit those dramatic drops without a second thought. And with smooth, domed buttons housed in felt bushings, every press feels effortless and solid.

Tone That Turns Heads

The polished metal ends don’t just look the part. They help project a strong, clear sound that holds up in crowded sessions. This is a concertina that cuts through the mix, with a tone that’s bright, rich, and full of character.

Built for Comfort

From the smooth, noise-free domed buttons to the soft, adjustable leather straps, every detail on the Skylark is designed for comfort and control. Play longer without cramping up, and find the fit that feels right, whether you’ve got large hands, small hands, or something in between.

Concertina That Grows With You

Ready to push your playing further? The Skylark is designed for serious players who want an instrument they won’t outgrow. With premium reeds, fast action, and durable construction, it’s the perfect step-up concertina for those moving beyond the basics.

What You Need on Day One

Every Skylark comes fully equipped for serious playing, including a deluxe hard case for safe transport and storage, 12 months of free access to our exclusive Irish Concertina Masterclass with expert Jack Talty, and free priority shipping so you can start playing sooner.

Built to Last

From the polished metal ends to the hand-finished reeds, every part of the Skylark is built for durability. The 7-fold leather bellows, riveted action, premium components, and professional set-up in our workshop ensure it will keep up with you for years of sessions and performances.

Air Control That Keeps You in the Moment

With its 7-fold leather bellows, the Skylark gives you more air to work with, allowing for expressive phrasing and dynamic control. Whether you’re playing a haunting slow air or a driving set of reels, you’ll have the breathing room to shape every phrase.

Who is the founder of McNeela Music?

McNeela Music was founded by Paraic McNeela, an Irish musician and enthusiast with a passion for providing high-quality musical instruments and accessories.

What modes of payments does McNeela Music accept?

McNeela Music offers a secure risk free payment experience. All Credit and Debit cards are accepted. Additionally, Paypal is also accepted at McNeela Music.

Does McNeela Music ship to my location?

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

When will my product be shipped?

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed. If you place an order on a weekend, it will be shipped on the next working day. Our goal is to ensure prompt delivery and get your musical instruments and accessories to you as quickly as possible. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on the destination and the shipping method selected during checkout.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

How can I track my order from McNeela Music?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email from McNeelaMusic. This email will contain a tracking number and instructions on how to track your package. If you cannot locate the email, please check your spam or junk folder as it may have been filtered there. If you still cannot find the tracking number, please reach out to our customer support at by Clicking Here.

What is the return policy at McNeela Music?

At McNeela Music, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality musical instruments and accessories. We offer a 30-day “Try-At-Home” return policy. We take great care in ensuring that our products are in excellent condition and carefully packed before being shipped.

Are the products at McNeela Music suitable for beginners?

Yes, McNeela Music caters to musicians of all skill levels, including beginners. They offer instruments and resources specifically designed to support newcomers on their musical journey.

How can I contact McNeela Music for further assistance?

For any further assistance, you can reach out to McNeela Music's customer support team through their website's contact page or by emailing us at service@mcneelamusic.com

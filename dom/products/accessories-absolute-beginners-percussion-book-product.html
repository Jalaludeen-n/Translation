Absolute Beginners Percussion Book

The Complete Guide to Playing Percussion

 

This expert tutorial book is ideal for those who want to expand their percussion playing and branch out into world or Latin percussion styles. With step by step instructions to guide you on your way and teach you everything you need to know, the Absolute Beginners Percussion Book makes learning percussion easier than ever before.

With free CD included featuring both demonstration and full-length accompaniment tracks, this is the perfect tutorial for beginner percussion players of any age.

Features of the Absolute Beginners Percussion Book

  • Easy to follow instructions for all ages
  • Step by step guidance using pictures
  • Suitable for those who don't read sheet music
  • Practical advice and tips
  • CD included

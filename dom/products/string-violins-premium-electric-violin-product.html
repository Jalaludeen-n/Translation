Beschreibung

The McNeela Premium Electric Violin

“Balanced, lightweight, absolutely love playing this violin!” - Robert S.

Here’s your chance to explore everything an electric violin has to offer. Whether you’re new to the instrument or an experienced professional, this eye-catching, lightweight violin is perfect for broadening your musical horizons.

Designed with a very distinctive cut-out body, this sleek and stylish design is given a high gloss black and red finish. Which has the added bonus of making it lighter and easier to handle.

It has an in-built pick-up and graphic equalizer (EQ) so you can plug it into an amplifier or a set of headphones, adjusting the volume and tone to suit your needs.  

Electric violins are particularly useful for practicing on as they're slient when not plugged in. Which allows you to practice fingering, difficult musical passages or bowing without waking sleeping babies or neighbours.  

This visually arresting violin is as impressive to listen to as it is to look at, and will turn heads wherever you play it.

Ready to Experiment?

An electric violin gives you huge scope for a wide range of experimentation. You can use loop pedals and other special effects to douse the sound you make with reverb or amp up the distortion, to play around with electronica, dance, folk, traditional and classical in all sorts of exciting and imaginative ways.

“My new McNeela Electric violin is an amazing find!The red finish is beautiful, it feels balanced and is very lightweight. The case is very high quality and it comes with everything needed (rosin/bow/cables). A real gem!” - Robert S.

All Quiet in the Home Front

Because electric violins produce almost no sound and are so much quieter than a muted acoustic violin, many acoustic players use one to practice on, even if they have no intention of ever actually plugging it in. If you really want to focus on your technique but are worried about your family or neighbours, an electric violin can be a huge help.

“Love it, I can practice quietly and the build quality is great.” - Neil M.

SEE ALL REVIEWS

Expand Your Bowing Technique

Producing the sound you want is easier on an electric violin because they typically require less heavy bowing, as the strings react in a different way to those on an acoustic violin. However, it’s important to keep practicing on an acoustic one as well, as your bowing technique can suffer if you don’t return to your acoustic instrument every now and then.

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on the destination and the shipping method selected during checkout.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

If you cannot locate the email, please check your spam or junk folder as it may have been filtered there. If you still cannot find the tracking number, please reach out to our customer support at by Clicking Here.

At McNeela Music, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality musical instruments and accessories. We offer a 30-day “Try-At-Home” return policy. We take great care in ensuring that our products are in excellent condition and carefully packed before being shipped.

Yes, McNeela Music caters to musicians of all skill levels, including beginners. They offer instruments and resources specifically designed to support newcomers on their musical journey.

Key Features of the Irish Flute Player‚Äôs Handbook:

  • Repair and maintenance guides on the Flute

This book gives you a step-by-step approach on learning the flute from scratch. In addition, the book includes instructionals on reading sheet music, fingering charts, an extended discography up to 1990, and includes bonus tracks from the Moneymusk CD. If you‚Äôre looking to expand your knowledge of the flute in every aspect, then this book is the guide for you.

S.C. Hamilton began his full-time professional career as a flute maker in 1979. He created his first workshop dedicated to crafting wooden simple system flutes for Irish music. Hamilton's contributions to the field have been significant and has been recognized for his expertise and dedication to traditional flute making.

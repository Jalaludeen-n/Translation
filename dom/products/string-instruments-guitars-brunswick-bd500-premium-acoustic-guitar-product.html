This is a versatile instrument that's both easy and comfortable to play. Its superb resonance and clarity of tone make it perfect for playing any style of music in any size venue.

The Brunswick BD500 offers a truly enjoyable playing experience. Once you pick it up you won't want to put it down!

The Iconic Dreadnought Body

The dreadnought guitar is an iconic shape with a large, broad body and squared shoulder. Dreadnought guitars are incredibly popular and have become the industry standard guitar shape throughout the world. They offer a big, bold sound with a powerful low end, making them highly versatile instruments that perfect for both strumming and fingerstyle playing.

The Brunswick 500 Series 

The Brunswick 500 series come with a solid spruce top as standard, and sapele back and sides, with satin finish. This combination offers the player a natural, earthy, rustic tone. These guitars feature heavier duty machine heads than the 200 series, offering tighter tuning stability and durability. 

About Brunswick Guitars

Established in London in 1995, Brunswick produce British-designed acoustic guitars and basses. Like McNeela Instruments, Brunswick's goal is to offer high quality instruments at affordable prices, offering exceptional value for money. Brunswick Guitars are suitable for players of all ages and levels, from young musicians and beginners all the way through to professional performing musicians. With over 25 years’ experience producing quality instruments that are not only easy to play but sound good too, Brunswick guitars are the go-to choice for thousands of guitar players around the world.

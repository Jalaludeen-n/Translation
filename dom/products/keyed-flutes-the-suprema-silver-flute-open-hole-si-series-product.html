Beschreibung

The McNeela "Suprema" Silver Plated Open Hole Concert Flute [SI SERIES]

The McNeela Suprema Concert Flute is a great choice for adult beginners, older students, or intermediate players ready to improve their skills.

It offers a rich, warm tone with a strong, clear sound in the high register. The keys feel responsive and easy to play, and the flute has good balance and weight.

With 16 open holes and a C footjoint, this flute is designed for those looking to explore more advanced techniques or upgrade from a basic model.

Features like the offset G key and split E mechanism make playing more comfortable and help with difficult high notes.

It also comes with tone hole plugs to support players who are still getting used to open holes.

The Suprema flute comes in a sturdy rosewood hard case with a soft velvet lining, plus a premium leather carry case for added protection.

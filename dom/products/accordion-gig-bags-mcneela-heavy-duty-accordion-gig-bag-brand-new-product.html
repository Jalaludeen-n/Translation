McNeela Heavy-Duty Accordion Gig Bag

At McNeela Music, we’re always thinking about the best ways of protecting your instruments from unnecessary wear and tear. That’s why we’re thrilled to announce our new and improved “McNeela Heavy-Duty Accordion Gig Bag.” This new gig bag combines heavy-duty polyester with a striking green and black design. Built to withstand regular use, this gig bag is perfect for musicians who need a reliable and stylish solution for keeping their accordions safe and secure.

Key Features

  • Heavy-Duty Polyester
  • Eye-Catching Green and Black Finish
  • Sturdy Clip-On Straps and Handle
  • Additional Backstraps
  • Front Pockets for Accessories
  • Available in 21 and 23 Button Models
  • Geared Towards Our 3-Voice Accordion Models
  • Dimensions: 13" X 13" X 6"

Why Choose the McNeela Heavy-Duty Accordion Gig Bag?

This sturdy gig bag is made to last and is a must-have for accordion players. Whether you're heading to a gig, a lesson, or just practicing away from home, the McNeela Heavy-Duty Accordion Gig Bag keeps your instrument protected and ready to go.

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed.

Product highlights

Unique Hand-Tuning System

Paraic has spent decades developing his trademark hand-tuning system so that it’s simple and easy for you to adjust your bodhrán’s sound and tone. This means you can react to the people around you and the music you’re playing, while you play. Which is particularly useful when you’re playing Irish trad.

“This is my second bodhran but it’s tuneable and it’s great to vary the sound. If you get a couple of different beaters, it helps to vary the sound.” Danny B.

Taped Around the Edges

All our bodhráns come taped around the edges, which helps to ‘dampen’ the tone, in much the same way that E rings do when used on a set of drums. This technique extends the harmonic range of the instrument and adds complexity to the bass tones.

Re-inforced Rim for Extra Strength

The four inch rim has an added layer of wood to provide extra strength while tuning, and there’s a removable T-bar and arm cut-out for extra playing comfort.

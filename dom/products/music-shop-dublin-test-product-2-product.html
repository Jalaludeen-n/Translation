 The Ultimate Rosewood 16” Deep-Rim Bodhrán Set

“More depth and power with the deeper rim”

The Ultimate Rosewood 16” is our best-selling bodhrán set. It has an extra deep rim of 5 ½ inches to give you added power and sustain, and features our unique McNeela 8-point tuning system.​

This allows you to tune as you play, effortlessly fine-tuning in response to the musicians around you, or to any changes in humidity or temperature, without interrupting your performance.

The bodhrán itself is edge-taped which extends its harmonics and adds complexity to the bass tones. Plus, it comes with an extensive selection of 6 beaters and weighted tippers, allowing you to explore a wide range of sounds.

Crafted from sustainably sourced, hand-stretched goatskin, this exquisitely made bodhrán produces a rich, resonant tone that's ideal for players of all levels.

Unique Tuning System

Our 8-point tuning system is effortlessly responsive and is easy to grip and fast to tune. This allows you to adjust your bodhrán whenever you need to so you can respond to any changes in humidity or temperature without interrupting your playing.

Taped Around the Edges

All our bodhráns come taped around the edges, which helps to ‘dampen’ the tone, in much the same way that E rings do when used on a set of drums. This technique extends the harmonic range of the instrument and adds complexity to the bass tones.

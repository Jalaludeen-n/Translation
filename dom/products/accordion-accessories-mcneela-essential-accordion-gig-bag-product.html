McNeela Essential Accordion Gig Bag

This McNeela Premium Accordion Gig Bag is the perfect option for both storing and transporting your Irish button accordion. It's cleverly designed for musicians on the go.

Transport your accordion with ease with this premium padded gig bag built to comfortably accommodate Irish traditional 21-button accordions.

NB: Fits button accordions up to 11"W x 10.5"H x 6"D (Black Dot size)

Key Features of the McNeela Essential Accordion Gig Bag:

  • Soft padded interior
  • Top Handle
  • Adjustable padded shoulder straps
  • Shock Proof Base
  • Rubber Floor Protectors
  • Zipper Closure
  • Additional front closure straps with side release buckle clips
  • Easy access front storage pocket
  • Side pocket information/travel tag storage
  • Black heavy duty polyester exterior

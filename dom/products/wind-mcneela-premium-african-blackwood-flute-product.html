Introducing the All-New Rich Tone Blackwood Flute

You don’t just want a flute, you want an instrument that responds to every nuance, that feels alive under your fingers. One that holds its own in the thick of a session and still rewards delicate phrasing with colour and richness. That’s the all-new Rich Tone Blackwood Flute.

Crafted from select cuts of richly grained African blackwood, this is a flute that gives back more than you put in.

We’ve taken elements from some of our most popular Irish flutes and added improvements like chamfered (chanter-style) indented finger holes, upgraded joint materials, and other design tweaks that help it deliver the kind of volume and playing experience advanced players will love.

Its Pratten-style bore brings the bold, resonant tone you need for crowded rooms, especially in the low end, but it stays nimble, expressive, and easy to play across both octaves.

The elliptical embouchure reacts instantly to changes in breath and angle, giving you tonal flexibility without strain.

The cork-lapped joints lock in tight with no air leaks, and the smooth tuning slide holds pitch reliably, even when the room temperature shifts mid-set.

This flute isn’t just powerful. It’s dependable, expressive, and ready for a lifetime of music.

Built for Power

At the heart of this flute is volume and control. With its Pratten-style bore and strong low D, it projects with confidence, even in a packed pub session. But unlike some other flutes, it doesn’t lose that colour when you pull back. It’s just as compelling in your living room as it is in a big setting.

Unmatched Sound & Playability

Known for its deep, resonant power, the low D note on this flute is particularly strong, making it ideal for traditional Irish music. With a Pratten-style bore, you’ll find that this note not only carries in a crowded session but also maintains a rich, full-bodied sound even when played softly.

Tune on the Fly

The built-in tuning slide holds firm without slipping and adjusts quickly, even in mixed temperature environments, perfect for adapting to other instruments, changes in weather, and different settings.

You’ll stay confidently in pitch without having to worry about it as you enjoy your favourite tunes.

It’s designed to grow with you and adapt to your style, offering a sound that evolves as your skills progress.

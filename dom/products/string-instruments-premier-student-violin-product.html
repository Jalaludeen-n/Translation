Product Description

McNeela Premier Student
Violin Electro Acoustic

 

The McNeela Premier Student Violin is a beautifully crafted instrument suitable for beginner and intermediate fiddle players of all levels. This premium student fiddle will help you on your musical journey, taking your playing to the next level.

A beautiful student violin, developed by musicians for musicians, the Premier has been one of our best selling instruments for many years. Here at McNeela Instruments we're always keen to improve. That's why our Premier Violin has undergone an update. This latest has been upgraded to feature a more dense spruce top, offering improved tone and volume, and adding to its robust design.

In addition to its dense spruce top, this violin features a maple back and sides with pear wood fingerboard for a strong, powerful tone and great volume. Our Premier Violin also comes with added pickup, transforming it into an electro acoustic violin that's ready to plug in and play.  

Comes with a free bow and hard case

Features of the McNeela Premier Student Violin

  • Electro acoustic violin
  • Pickup with audio output jack
  • Solid hand carved spruce top
  • Solid hand carved maple back and sides
  • Beautiful inlaid purfling
  • Satin antique finish
  • Pearwood fingerboard
  • Jujube wood chin rest and pegs
  • Alloy tailpiece with 4 fine tuners
  • Full size (4/4)

 Standout Qualities of the McNeela Premier Student Violin

  • Excellent volume, assisted by pickup
  • Responsive action
  • Clear, powerful tone
  • Free Brazilwood bow and rosin
  • Free violin case
  • Suitable for players of all ages and abilities

Luxury Quality at an Affordable Price

At McNeela Instruments we are committed to providing customers with the highest standard of instruments at the most accessible prices. Our low cost, high quality instruments are designed to assist our customers to achieve their goals. So, if you're looking for a high quality violin for sale at an affordable price, you've come to the right place.

While most quality instruments suitable for those at an intermediate level cost upwards of €500, The Premier is an exception, offering top quality at an unbeatable price.

Sleek and Understated

The sleek spruce top offers an understated natural look finish with beautiful inlaid purfling. This is a violin that looks as good as it sounds.

Quality Sound

The quality of the strings is one of the factors that has the greatest impact on the sound the violin produces. The playability, sound and quality of a violin all depend on the strings that it’s fitted with. We only use the best quality strings for our instruments, including those designed for beginner musicians. We want to enable you to sound good, right from your very first note.

The McNeela Premier Student Violin features a variety of quality tonewoods which produce a strong, resonant tone and excellent response. These materials offer a clear, bright, powerful tone and plenty of volume.

Learn more about how to choose the right violin strings.

Electro Acoustic

One of the most exciting features of the McNeela Premier Student Violin is its audio jack output. A pickup means volume is never an issue. Just plug in and play and control the volume and tone levels directly from the control panel.

An electric acoustic violin offers the best of both worlds. Electric violins offer huge scope for experimenting with sound. They are perfect for any dance, electronica, pop or rock enthusiasts out there. You can use them with loop pedals and other special effects, dousing them in reverb or turning up the distortion to really rock out.

Electric violins offer a range of creative features for any violinist who is looking to expand their range.

Learn more about the exciting benefits of electric violins.

Built to Last

The McNeela Premier Student Violin has been built with durability in mind, making it a reliable long lasting investment. As the strings are exercised, it will continue to improve with age.

To completely put your mind at ease however, we proudly offer a one year guarantee on all new violins for sale, against any material defects arising from the product. Beyond one year, we will still happily service your instrument, should the need arise.

Additionally, if you're unsatisfied for any reason whatsoever, we offer a full unconditional money-back guarantee for 14 days from the day you receive your McNeela instrument.

Elevate Your Playing

The McNeela Premier Violin is the perfect fit for the intermediate to advanced fiddle player. This premium Irish fiddle is carefully designed with ease of play in mind, enabling the player to take full control and play with confidence. It’s crafted to the highest standard and perfect for students who are ready to advance and master their instrument.

Who is the founder of McNeela Music?

McNeela Music was founded by Paraic McNeela, an Irish musician and enthusiast with a passion for providing high-quality musical instruments and accessories.

What modes of payments does McNeela Music accept?

McNeela Music offers a secure risk free payment experience. All Credit and Debit cards are accepted. Additionally, Paypal is also accepted at McNeela Music.

Does McNeela Music ship to my location?

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

When will my product be shipped?

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed. If you place an order on a weekend, it will be shipped on the next working day. Our goal is to ensure prompt delivery and get your musical instruments and accessories to you as quickly as possible. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on the destination and the shipping method selected during checkout.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

How can I track my order from McNeela Music?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email from McNeelaMusic. This email will contain a tracking number and instructions on how to track your package. If you cannot locate the email, please check your spam or junk folder as it may have been filtered there. If you still cannot find the tracking number, please reach out to our customer support at by Clicking Here.

What is the return policy at McNeela Music?

At McNeela Music, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality musical instruments and accessories. We offer a 30-day “Try-At-Home” return policy. We take great care in ensuring that our products are in excellent condition and carefully packed before being shipped.

Are the products at McNeela Music suitable for beginners?

Yes, McNeela Music caters to musicians of all skill levels, including beginners. They offer instruments and resources specifically designed to support newcomers on their musical journey.

How can I contact McNeela Music for further assistance?

For any further assistance, you can reach out to McNeela Music's customer support team through their website's contact page or by emailing us at service@mcneelamusic.com

Beschreibung

 The McNeela Antique Finish Premium Student Tenor Saxophone Set

Explore the Fusion of Classic Beauty and Modern Playability with the McNeela Antique Finish Premium Tenor Saxophone Set. Tailored for musicians who value both style and performance, this set highlights a remarkable ancient colour finish. Enhanced with Italy keys cushioning on the mouthpiece for superior comfort and protection, it promises a standout appearance coupled with unparalleled ease of use for players across all skill levels. Delight in the deep, resonant tones of an instrument that redefines excellence.

This bundle is thoughtfully equipped with a high-quality soft case, reinforced with belts for extra protection, making it perfect for both secure storage and travel. Comfort during play is ensured by the inclusion of a durable neck strap, designed to reduce strain during extended practice sessions or performances.

To maintain the pristine condition of your instrument and ensure optimal performance, the bundle includes a comprehensive set of maintenance accessories: gloves for handling, a strap for secure transport, lubricant for smooth operation, along with a cleaning cloth and brush for regular upkeep.

Furthermore, this all-encompassing bundle provides a pack of 10 reeds, catering to the diverse needs and preferences of saxophonists, alongside a tuner to aid in achieving the perfect pitch for every performance.

Who is the founder of McNeela Music?

McNeela Music was founded by Paraic McNeela, an Irish musician and enthusiast with a passion for providing high-quality musical instruments and accessories.

What modes of payments does McNeela Music accept?

McNeela Music offers a secure risk free payment experience. All Credit and Debit cards are accepted. Additionally, Paypal is also accepted at McNeela Music.

Does McNeela Music ship to my location?

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

When will my product be shipped?

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed. If you place an order on a weekend, it will be shipped on the next working day. Our goal is to ensure prompt delivery and get your musical instruments and accessories to you as quickly as possible. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on the destination and the shipping method selected during checkout.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

How can I track my order from McNeela Music?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email from McNeelaMusic. This email will contain a tracking number and instructions on how to track your package. If you cannot locate the email, please check your spam or junk folder as it may have been filtered there. If you still cannot find the tracking number, please reach out to our customer support at by Clicking Here.

What is the return policy at McNeela Music?

At McNeela Music, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality musical instruments and accessories. We offer a 30-day “Try-At-Home” return policy. We take great care in ensuring that our products are in excellent condition and carefully packed before being shipped.

Are the products at McNeela Music suitable for beginners?

Yes, McNeela Music caters to musicians of all skill levels, including beginners. They offer instruments and resources specifically designed to support newcomers on their musical journey.

How can I contact McNeela Music for further assistance?

For any further assistance, you can reach out to McNeela Music's customer support team through their website's contact page or by emailing us at service@mcneelamusic.com

Premium Sunset Mandolin

Elevate your mandolin abilities with our stunning, brand-new McNeela Premium Sunset Mandolin. This mandolin is a beautifully crafted instrument, designed with visual appeal and, most importantly, gorgeous tones.

It’s ideal for beginners and intermediate players looking to advance to the upper levels of mandolin playing.

  • High-Grade Machine Heads for Reliable Tuning

Premium Sunset Mandolin Product Hightlights

F-Hole Mandolins vs Oval Hole Mandolins

Aside from the aesthetically pleasing differences, F-hole mandolins will usually have a brighter shimmery tone with more volume and a sharper attack in comparison to their oval hole counterparts. As a result, F-hole mandolins tend to offer a more percussive sound. 

However, the higher the craftsmanship is, the less differences in sound quality are.

Superior Craftsmanship and Durability

At McNeela, we take great pride in the high grade materials we use in our instruments, especially in this one, which features a spruce top with flamed maple sides and back, and an ebony fretboard and bridge to boot. The result is an instrument that feels as good as it looks, designed for comfort and built to last. Its clean natural finish makes a stunning visual statement that’s designed to reflect the player’s musical passion.

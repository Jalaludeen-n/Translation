Aroma TDX-22 Electric Drum Kit

Aroma TDX-22 Electric Drum Kit

 
This 8 piece kit consists of 5 mesh head drums and 3 half silicone cymbals, offering a natural feel and ultra quiet response. Snare, bass, toms, hi hat, crash & ride cymbals are all mounted on a sturdy 4-post aluminium rack, including hi hat and bass drum pedals.
 
This kit is perfect for those who love the drums but don't love noise - plug in your headphones and learn to play in complete silence.

Product title

Product highlights

Sound Like Your Favourite Kits!

The Aroma TDX-22 offers endless options for student musicians.

Choose from 144 onboard preset sounds, play along with the inbuilt metronome, play your favourite tracks using the auxiliary input, or record using the Midi and USB outputs - the choice is yours.

 

Control Your Volume Easily!

With the Aroma Electric Drum Kit volume is never an issue. You can amplify your drums by simply plugging into a speaker.

Alternatively, if you’re worried about noise complaints, plug in headphones to practise silently. Your neighbours will thank you!

 

KEY FEATURES

  • 5 Drums: Snare x1, Kick x1, Toms x3
  • 3 Cymbals: 1 Crash, 1 Ride and Hi Hat Pedals
  • Kick Drum Pedal
  • 20 preset drum sounds
  • 9v Power
  • Durable construction for long-lasting use
  • 144 Sounds Total
  • Metronome
  • Bluetooth
  • Audio output
  • Headphone Jack Input
  • USB MIDI

Your Instructor

Tom Doorley is a master Irish flute player, from Glenageary, Co. Dublin. 

Well versed in both classical and traditional Irish music, Tom’s early influences include Vincent broderick, Micheál Ó hAlmhain, Cormac Brathnach, the late Sámus MacMathuna and of course, the iconic Matt Molloy. 

An All-Ireland Irish flute champion, Tom continued his studies in classical flute, qualifying with an honours bachelor degree in music performance from Dublin Institute of Technology and Trinity College Dublin. Tom further developed his flute playing while studying with acclaimed flautists including Julius Baker, James Galway and Marzio Conti in the Boccherini Conservatoire in Lucca, Italy.

Widely regarded as a master flautist, Tom is renowned for his expressive tone, and unique, often playful musical interpretations of traditional Irish tunes which include wildly creative melodic variations. His technical prowess is rivalled by few. 

In 1996 Tom joined the internationally acclaimed Irish music ensemble, Danú.  He performed professionally with the band for fourteen years, recording a number of critically acclaimed

When it comes to learning music, you can’t beat the guidance of an expert teacher and when planning this exciting new series, I knew there was only one man for the job - Tom Doorley. 

The greatest Irish flute player since Matt Molloy - Tom is a master of the Irish flute renowned for his expressive tone, unique musical interpretations and creative melodic variations. He has taken the Irish music world by storm and his technical prowess is rivaled by few. You couldn’t ask for a better teacher.

From Tom Dooley:

“I consider myself very lucky to be grounded in two rich flute playing traditions. I’ve been immersed in traditional Irish music my whole life - playing the Irish wooden flute from a very young age - but I also went on to study classical flute at third level with the legendary Marzio Conti in the Boccherini Conservatoire in Lucca, Italy. Marzio was a true master of his instrument & I learned so much from him - so many great techniques that I have applied to my own flute playing over the years. 

When I play the flute these two musical worlds collide, but my flute playing is all the better for it. 

Throughout this course I hope to share the best of both with you, teaching you exercises that will drastically improve your flute playing and equipping you with all the skills you need to become a truly great flute player. 

The Irish flute is one of the greatest instruments in the world and I’m looking forward to helping you discover all it has to offer. I’m so excited to lead you on this musical journey.” - TOM

What You'll Learn

This course offers an in depth exploration of Irish flute playing, leaving you with a comprehensive understanding of the most critical elements of flute playing.

 

Anyone can teach a tune. Tom’s lessons offer more than that. Under his expert guidance you’ll learn everything you need to know about flute playing technique - and not just the basics. This innovative 3 part course, will take you from beginner, right through intermediate, and all the way to advanced level.

 

This exciting flute course grants you access to:

  • Beginner, Intermediate & Advanced Masterclasses
  • 30 Immersive Traditional Irish flute Lessons
  • Exclusive bonus classes
  • Over 30 great flute tunes to learn
  • World class tuition with leading flute expert Tom Doorly
  • Complete access to our Beginner, Intermediate & Advanced flute classes
  • Exclusive Bonus Materials plus Insider Tips
  • Flexible lessons that you can pause, slow down, speed up, follow at your own pace and bring with you wherever you go. 

Explore breathing techniques, tone, musical expression and much more and, most importantly, learn how to apply these techniques to every tune you learn. Tom’s tried and tested teaching methods guarantee results!

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

When will my product be shipped?

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed. Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

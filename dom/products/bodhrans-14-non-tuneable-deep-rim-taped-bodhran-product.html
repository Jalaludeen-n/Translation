Beschreibung

14" Non-Tunable Deep Rim Bodhrán by Paraic McNeela

Top Benefits:

  • A Deep, Rich Sound: Deep rim design enhances sound projection for a fuller, more dynamic tone.
  • Compact and Portable: 14-inch diameter makes it easy to transport and play, perfect for both home and sessions.
  • Low Maintenance: Fixed tuning means less hassle with setup, so you can focus on playing.
  • Crafted from Mulberry Wood: High-quality mulberry wood offers durability and a warm, traditional tone.
  • Additional Accessories: Supplied with a hardwood bodhrán beater and protective gig bag.

The 14" Non-Tunable Deep Rim Bodhrán by Paraic McNeela is built to offer a rich, deep sound in a compact, portable design. Crafted from mulberry wood, this bodhrán combines durability with a warm and deep tone. The deep rim construction ensures excellent sound projection, while the taped edges provide extra tonal depth and control. This bodhrán is perfect for musicians who need a reliable, high-quality instrument without the need for tuning adjustments.

A Powerful, Deep Sound

With its deep rim design, this bodhrán delivers a fuller sound, ideal for both solo performances and group sessions. The taped edges give you a more refined sound and provides flexibility in your playing.

Compact and Easy to Carry

Measuring 14 inches in diameter, this bodhrán is lightweight and portable, making it easy to take with you to sessions or performances. Despite its smaller size, it still provides excellent sound projection and depth.

Low Maintenance

Designed with fixed tuning, this bodhrán requires no adjustments, making it a great choice for players who prefer a hassle-free instrument. Simply pick it up and play without worrying about tuning.

Crafted from Quality Mulberry Wood

Made from mulberry wood, this bodhrán offers both durability and a traditional, warm tone. The high-quality wood construction ensures a solid feel and long-lasting performance.

Complete with Accessories

The bodhrán comes with a durable hardwood beater for comfortable playing and a gig bag for easy transport and protection. The gig bag helps shield your instrument from scratches and damage, keeping it in excellent condition.

Features & Specs:

  • Deep Rim: For enhanced sound projection.
  • Fixed Tuning: A simple, no-adjustment design for easy playing.
  • 14-Inch Diameter: Compact and portable without sacrificing sound quality.
  • Taped Edges: Adds control and depth to the sound.
  • Mulberry Tonewood: Durable and warm-toned wood for long-lasting use.
  • Supplied with Hardwood Beater: Comfortable and durable for traditional playing.
  • Includes Gig Bag: Protects the bodhrán during transport and storage.

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

Beschreibung

Brunswick BD200 Acoustic Guitar
 

Finished with an eye catching deep red gloss, the BD200 offers bold looks and a bold sound to match. This dreadnought guitar produces a big sound with a rich tone and plenty of resonance. Its dimensions and design allow the instrument to project its sound very effectively, being able to deliver a loud but balanced tone and be heard as part of a group - perfect for playing in a traditional Irish music session.

Dreadnought guitars are incredibly popular and have become the industry standard guitar shape throughout the world - perfect for those who want a traditional appearance and great performance. The Brunswick BD200 is a quality instrument, offering superb affordability for those looking to buy their first acoustic guitar.

Features of the Brunswick BD200:

  • Spruce Top
  • Sapele Back and Sides
  • Red Gloss Finish
  • Enclosed Die-Cast Machine heads
  • Composite Technical Wood Fingerboard and Bridge

Bonus Features:

  • Comes with free guitar gig bag

Fingerboard Radius 16”

About The Maker

Established in London in 1995, Brunswick produce British-designed acoustic guitars and basses. Like McNeela Instruments, Brunswick's goal is to offer high quality instruments at affordable prices, offering exceptional value for money. Brunswick Guitars are suitable for players of all ages and levels, from young musicians and beginners all the way through to professional performing musicians. With over 25 years’ experience producing quality instruments that are not only easy to play but sound good too, Brunswick guitars are the go-to choice for thousands of guitar players around the world.

The Hidersine Vivente comes in four different sizes: 1/4, 1/2, 3/4 and Full size (4/4)

Most adults and children over the age of twelve play with a full size (4/4) violin. Smaller sizes are generally used by children who can‚Äôt yet stretch down the fingerboard.

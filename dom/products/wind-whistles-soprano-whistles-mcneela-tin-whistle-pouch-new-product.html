McNeela Tin Whistle Pouch
 

Our new and improved McNeela Tin Whistle Pouch can comfortably fit two soprano tin whistles, up to C whistle size. It's ideal for keeping your whistles looking and sounding like new!

Key Features of the McNeela Tin Whistle Pouch

  • Fits two soprano whistles up to C size 
  • Velcro closure
  • Tough protective canvas exterior
  • Lightly padded for extra protection

Protect your whistles from unwanted knock and bumps, and help reduce tarnishing with this premium McNeela Tin Whistle Pouch.

  • 1 year warranty

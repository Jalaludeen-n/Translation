Beschreibung

The McNeela Original 2 Voice B/C Button Accordion

“Sounds great, feels great, plays great!” - James H.

Our 2 voice, button accordion in B/C is a clasic, traditional accordion in a sturdy, durable build.

It's aimed at beginners and intermediate players, and comes with 21 or 23 buttons.

A 21 button accordion is lighter to hold and simpler to play, making it ideal for smaller stature players.

While the 23 button model gives you a wider repertoire of tunes, and suits players confident they’ll progress from beginner to Intermediate and beyond.

It’s built with Czech-made reeds which produce a bright, clear tone with plenty of volume.

And it has large, smooth pearlescent buttons on a flat fingerboard with a thumb groove, and comes with adjustable hand and shoulder straps. 

Whether you choose the 21 or the 23 button accordion, this instrument’s rich, layered sound and responsiveness make learning the accordion a daily pleasure.

Product highlights

Comfortable and Easy to Play

Your accordion has large, smooth pearlescent buttons, an adjustable bass strap and a thumb grooved fingerboard for ease of play. This reduces any strain and means you can focus more fully on the music you’re playing.

“This is a splendid little accordion with great tone and action! The course that comes with it is very helpful and offers lots of good advice. Strongly recommended.” Frank R.

A Choice of 21 or 23 Buttons

This is a two row, diatonic button accordion in the key of B/C. All that means is that the outer row of buttons are in the key of B, and the ones on the inner row are in C. While the 21 button model is a little bit easier to play, and the 23 button model gives you access to a slightly bigger repertoire of tunes.

“Great looking instrument with awesome sound!” Scott K.

Quality Sound & Superior Finish

This accordion is made from an American cherrywood frame and finish, with carefully selected, hand-crafted, Czech-made reeds and a Paolo Soprani bass layout. It’s a premium accordion crafted with quality materials that’s designed and made to last.

McNeela Music was founded by Paraic McNeela, an Irish musician and enthusiast with a passion for providing high-quality musical instruments and accessories.

McNeela Music offers a secure risk free payment experience. All Credit and Debit cards are accepted. Additionally, Paypal is also accepted at McNeela Music.

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

When will my product be shipped?

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed. If you place an order on a weekend, it will be shipped on the next working day. Our goal is to ensure prompt delivery and get your musical instruments and accessories to you as quickly as possible. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on the destination and the shipping method selected during checkout.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

If you cannot locate the email, please check your spam or junk folder as it may have been filtered there. If you still cannot find the tracking number, please reach out to our customer support at by Clicking Here.

What is the return policy at McNeela Music?

At McNeela Music, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality musical instruments and accessories. We offer a 30-day “Try-At-Home” return policy. We take great care in ensuring that our products are in excellent condition and carefully packed before being shipped.

Are the products at McNeela Music suitable for beginners?

Yes, McNeela Music caters to musicians of all skill levels, including beginners. They offer instruments and resources specifically designed to support newcomers on their musical journey.

Beschreibung

Centre Ribbed Ebony Beater

Crafted from beautiful ebony, this beater is a versatile addition to any bodhrán player’s toolkit. The ribbed centre provides excellent grip and control, making it particularly well-suited for triplet-style playing and other fast, rhythmic techniques.

Ideal for double-ended use, it delivers clear, crisp pops while offering comfort and balance in the hand. A reliable, go-to option for players who want both speed and stability in their playing.

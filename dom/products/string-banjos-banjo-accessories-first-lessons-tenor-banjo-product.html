Beschreibung

First Lessons Tenor Banjo

Using classic favourites, Joe Carr teaches the basics of accompaniment, basic melody and the popular chord melody style.

Chords and melody are combined to produce professional sounding solo arrangements.

Includes play-along practice tracks that speed up the learning process. It doesn't get any easier!

What You'll Learn:

  • The Tenor Banjo and Its Parts
  • Holding the Banjo
  • The Pick
  • Hand Placement
  • Tuning
  • The F & C Chords
  • Using the Book
  • Strumming
  • Picking

Includes the following American banjo folk classics:

Skip To My Lou, Aura Lee, Wait Til the Sun Shines Nellie, Hello My Baby, Ballin the Jack The World Is Waiting For the Sunrise, What a Friend, Chinatown, My Chinatown, Peg Of My Heart

Product highlights

The Bodhrán That Took 50 Years to Perfect

It all started in 1973, when I built a bodhrán for my band’s singer. What followed was five decades of obsession, spent learning from Ireland’s finest, gathering feedback at sessions and in my workshop, chasing the perfect tone.

This isn’t a drum “inspired” by tradition. It is tradition, shaped and sharpened by real-world playing. It feels right the moment you pick it up, and sounds better with every tune. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned session player, it’s built to grow with you.

“Great sound and playability. Brilliantly crafted and gorgeous to look at.”- Andrew A.

SEE ALL REVIEWS

Solid Rosewood and a Deep Rim for Rich Tone

Rosewood isn’t just beautiful — it’s one of the most sought-after tonewoods for percussion. Its density and grain structure provide exceptional resonance and projection, creating a bodhrán tone that’s rich, complex, and naturally amplified.

The extra depth of the 5.5" rim enhances bass resonance and tonal sustain, giving your playing a powerful, room-filling volume and a broader dynamic expression.

“I am thrilled with my bodhrán - it has such a deep, rich tone, and I love the tuning control. The assortment of tippers that were included in the set is great as well.” - Cole Q.

SEE ALL REVIEWS

Hand-Selected Goatskin

Each goatskin is carefully chosen for tone and texture, then hand-cured and stretched for maximum musical responsiveness. Sustainably sourced and untreated by chemicals, these skins deliver a balanced mix of depth, clarity, and sensitivity to touch.

And you can maintain the longevity and responsiveness of your goatskin with the included DrumDiddly bodhrán cream, to condition and protect the skin for years of consistent tone and feel.

“It is just what I needed to continue my musical journey. I am still exploring its brilliant tonal range.” - Stanley P.

8-Point Precision Hand-Tuning System

Humidity and travel are no match for the 8-point hand-tuning system, which lets you easily fine-tune your bodhrán on the fly. Designed for fast access and smooth adjustment, you can keep your tone tight, clear, and ready for anything.

“It looks fabulous and sounds even better. The head is very high quality and the tuning mechanism is simple and functional. The array of beaters is awesome and the gig bag is very nice.” -Mark S.

Taped Edges for Harmonic Control

The bodhrán’s taped edges extend its harmonic range and add warmth and focus to each stroke. This time-tested technique reduces overtones, increases sustain, and brings out the best in your tone whether you’re playing softly or going full volume.

“My new bodhran is absolutely a work of art. It’s beautifully made and sounds incredible. Took it to its first session and got positive feedback. Highly recommend.” - Marie C.

Complete Beater & Tipper Collection

This set includes six handcrafted beaters and tippers, from weighted to ribbed to hotrod styles. Whether you're crafting a delicate waltz or powering through a jig, you’ll have the tools to match every musical moment.

Premium Gig Bag Included for Protection and Portability

Your bodhrán comes with a padded gig bag built for daily use. Whether you're traveling to a session, performing on stage, or storing your drum at home, your instrument stays protected from wear and tear.

Performance You Can Count On

The Renegade 5-String Banjo 


“It’s a work of art. Beautiful instrument and an amazing sound. I’m new to the banjo but the quality makes me want to keep practising.” - John C.

The all-new Renegade Banjo isn’t just any 5-string, it’s a carefully crafted instrument with deep musical roots, modern comfort, and everything you need to begin confidently.

It’s specifically designed for banjo beginners and musicians looking to expand their instruments and genres.

Made with a warm-toned mahogany resonator, the Renegade produces a mellow, full-bodied sound that catches your ear right away.

You’ll find the 22-fret neck and rosewood fingerboard responsive and easy to play, and the planetary tuners and brackets make it easy to keep playing your best.

Built on quality and big on style, it’s the perfect choice for all your favourite tunes, from old-time to bluegrass, folk, and beyond.

Product highlights

Confidence from the First Strum

The Renegade’s smooth rosewood fingerboard and quality D'Addario strings give unbeatable playability and responsive action, making it ideal for first-time players. It has a beautiful finish and solid build, so you won't have to fight the finicky parts or unknown rattles that can plague other beginner banjos.

Whether it’s your first instrument or you’ve been playing a while, you’ll start building confidence from the moment you pick it up.

Playability that Makes Practice Easy

The neck, fingerboard, and strings are designed to work together and keep you comfortable during practice sessions. Fingerpicking a slow air at home or strumming along with friends, you'll love how simple it is to learn quickly and keep at it.

Why It Sounds So Warm

The mahogany resonator and Remo head work together to produce a mellow, full-bodied tone with just the right amount of brightness. A quality resonator projects your sound outward, making it perfect for sessions, performances, and jam rooms. Every note rings out clearly.

Built for Bluegrass and Beyond

Ready for your favourite bluegrass, old-time, folk, and traditional tunes, this is a 5-string you won’t outgrow. The Gibson-inspired design, smooth finish, and classic silhouette mean it looks the part at any setting, while the attention to detail and high-quality materials keep it light, balanced, and easy to handle.

Everything You Need and More

Get off to a solid start quickly with our Premium Banjo Set, included FREE with your Freedom Banjo!

The digital tuner makes tuning fast and simple every time, spare strings keep you playing, and the banjo strap and premium padded case keep your instrument safe and accessible anytime, anywhere.

Finally, your copy of Hal Leonard’s Banjo Method Book 1 has all the music know-how you need to start mastering your new instrument right away. All valued at more than €100/$100!

Crisp, Clear, and Easily in Tune

Its 2-footed bridge adds extra brightness and clarity to every note to help your playing stand out without ever sounding harsh. Then the Renegade’s 30-bracket design and 4:1 geared planetary tuners mean you can adjust on the spot and stay there tune after tune. Even as a beginner player, you’ll be able to tap into a sound all your own right away.

Musician Setup Makes All the Difference

Each Renegade Banjo is professionally set up by our workshop team before it leaves McNeela. That means smooth action, quality you can count on, and a banjo that plays clean right out of the box. No buzzing or guesswork. Just great music.

McNeela Music offers a secure risk free payment experience. All Credit and Debit cards are accepted. Additionally, Paypal is also accepted at McNeela Music.

Does McNeela Music ship to my location?

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on the destination and the shipping method selected during checkout.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email from McNeelaMusic. This email will contain a tracking number and instructions on how to track your package. If you cannot locate the email, please check your spam or junk folder as it may have been filtered there. If you still cannot find the tracking number, please reach out to our customer support at by Clicking Here.

What is the return policy at McNeela Music?

At McNeela Music, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality musical instruments and accessories. We offer a 30-day “Try-At-Home” return policy. We take great care in ensuring that our products are in excellent condition and carefully packed before being shipped.

Are the products at McNeela Music suitable for beginners?

Yes, McNeela Music caters to musicians of all skill levels, including beginners. They offer instruments and resources specifically designed to support newcomers on their musical journey.

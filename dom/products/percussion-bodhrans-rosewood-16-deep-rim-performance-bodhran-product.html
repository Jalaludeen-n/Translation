Beschreibung

The Rosewood 16” Deep Rim Performance Bodhrán

“Brilliant look, feel and sound” - Ocean C.

This 16” bodhrán is crafted from Indian rosewood as this quality tonewood produces a particularly rich and expansive tone. And we’ve given it an extra deep rim of 5 ½ inches to give you added power, sustain and volume.

It features Paraic’s unique 8-point tuning system which we’ve devised to make it as easy as possible for you to fine-tune your bodhrán as you play.

It’s made using sustainably sourced, hand-stretched goatskin and the bodhrán itself is taped around the edges and this extends its harmonics and adds complexity to the bass tones.

This beautifully made, hand-crafted bodhrán produces rich, deep tones and a resonant sustain and is the ideal instrument to sit in on an Irish trad session.

Product highlights

The Bodhrán That Took 50 Years to Perfect

It all started in 1973, when I built a bodhrán for my band’s singer. What followed was five decades of obsession, spent learning from Ireland’s finest, gathering feedback at sessions and in my workshop, chasing the perfect tone.

This isn’t a drum “inspired” by tradition. It is tradition, shaped and sharpened by real-world playing. It feels right the moment you pick it up, and sounds better with every tune. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned session player, it’s built to grow with you.

“Great sound and playability. Brilliantly crafted and gorgeous to look at.” - Andrew A.

Solid Rosewood and a Deep Rim for Rich Tone

Rosewood isn’t just beautiful, it’s one of the most sought-after tonewoods for percussion. Its density and grain structure provide exceptional resonance and projection, creating a bodhrán tone that’s rich, complex, and naturally amplified.

The extra depth of the 5.5" rim enhances bass resonance and tonal sustain, giving your playing a powerful, room-filling volume and a broader dynamic expression.

“Really love learning the bodhrán on this drum! It inspires me to play better, with its incredible quality.” - Lou F.

SEE ALL REVIEWS

Hand-Selected Goatskin

Each goatskin is carefully chosen for tone and texture, then hand-cured and stretched for maximum musical responsiveness. Sustainably sourced and untreated by chemicals, these skins deliver a balanced mix of depth, clarity, and sensitivity to touch.

“It is just what I needed to continue my musical journey. I am still exploring its brilliant tonal range.” - Stanley P.

8-Point Precision Hand-Tuning System

Humidity and travel are no match for the 8-point hand-tuning system, which lets you easily fine-tune your bodhrán on the fly. Designed for fast access and smooth adjustment, you can keep your tone tight, clear, and ready for anything.

“My new bodhrán is absolutely a work of art. It’s beautifully made and sounds incredible. Took it to its first session and got positive feedback. Highly recommend.” - Marie C.

Taped Edges for Harmonic Control

The bodhrán’s taped edges extend its harmonic range and add warmth and focus to each stroke. This time-tested technique reduces overtones, increases sustain, and brings out the best in your tone whether you’re playing softly or going full volume.

“It looks fabulous and sounds even better. The head is very high quality and the tuning mechanism is simple and functional. The gig bag is very nice.” - Mark S.

Tone That Turns Heads

This bodhrán sings under the tipper. From delicate rolls to bold accents, it delivers a rich, resonant tone with fast attack and plenty of sustain. The goatskin head is carefully treated to retain natural variation and character, giving you a truly musical drum that sounds alive.

Who is the founder of McNeela Music?

McNeela Music was founded by Paraic McNeela, an Irish musician and enthusiast with a passion for providing high-quality musical instruments and accessories.

What modes of payments does McNeela Music accept?

McNeela Music offers a secure risk free payment experience. All Credit and Debit cards are accepted. Additionally, Paypal is also accepted at McNeela Music.

Does McNeela Music ship to my location?

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

When will my product be shipped?

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed. If you place an order on a weekend, it will be shipped on the next working day. Our goal is to ensure prompt delivery and get your musical instruments and accessories to you as quickly as possible. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on the destination and the shipping method selected during checkout.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

How can I track my order from McNeela Music?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email from McNeelaMusic. This email will contain a tracking number and instructions on how to track your package. If you cannot locate the email, please check your spam or junk folder as it may have been filtered there. If you still cannot find the tracking number, please reach out to our customer support at by Clicking Here.

What is the return policy at McNeela Music?

At McNeela Music, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality musical instruments and accessories. We offer a 30-day “Try-At-Home” return policy. We take great care in ensuring that our products are in excellent condition and carefully packed before being shipped.

Are the products at McNeela Music suitable for beginners?

Yes, McNeela Music caters to musicians of all skill levels, including beginners. They offer instruments and resources specifically designed to support newcomers on their musical journey.

How can I contact McNeela Music for further assistance?

For any further assistance, you can reach out to McNeela Music's customer support team through their website's contact page or by emailing us at service@mcneelamusic.com

The McNeela Flute & Whistle Combo Hard Case

The McNeela Flute and Whistle Combo Case is the perfect solution for players who want secure, compact storage for both their flute and whistles.

Designed for gigging musicians, session players, and travelling performers, this sturdy hard case keeps your instruments safe and protected — all in one neat package.

Made from tough injection-moulded resin and padded with high-density foam, the case features custom compartments for your flute and up to three whistles (D, C, and Bb).

Lightweight yet shock absorbent, it's built to handle the bumps of everyday travel while keeping your gear in top condition.

Whether you're carrying a treasured wooden flute or a full whistle set, this case offers reliable protection and smart design.

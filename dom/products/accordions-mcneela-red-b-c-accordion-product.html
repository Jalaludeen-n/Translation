 The McNeela Red B/C Accordion

“Looks beautiful and sounds great.” - Daniel L.

This is a 21 button, 2 voice accordion that’s been designed with beginners and students in mind. The 2 rows of buttons are tuned to B/C, with the outer row of buttons tuned to B and the inner ones to C.

This set-up makes all the notes on the chromatic scale available to you and allows you to play a wide variety of tunes in a number of keys.

B/C tuning to the most popular accordion tuning for traditional Irish music as it allows for a particularly flowing style of playing.  

It’s built with Czech-made ‘Tipo a Mano’ reeds, as these reeds are particularly well suited for the sounds associated with Irish trad.

And it has large, smooth pearlescent buttons on a flat fingerboard with a thumb groove, and comes with adjustable hand and shoulder straps for added comfort.

This beautifully crafted, sturdy instrument is effortlessly responsive and is the perfect entry-level accordion for students of all levels.

Product highlights

Easy to Play

Thanks to their compact size, smaller accordions are easier to play. Because the air is more compressed in the smaller bellows, the air gets delivered to the reeds at a higher pressure. This makes it easier to play at a louder volume and means you don’t have to work so hard to pump the bellows.

“Really like this instrument, it’s a pleasure to play.” - Emma B.

Quality Sound, Big Volume

The playability, sound and tone of an accordion all depend on the reeds it’s fitted with, and this accordion is fitted with Czech-made Tipo a Mano dural reeds. These quality reeds produce a clear, bright, powerful tone with plenty of volume, making them ideal for playing Irish trad.

“Great sound (super reeds), well built, good finish. Would be proud to play this box anywhere. A great find.” - Charles M.

Built for Comfort

The flat fingerboard and thumb groove, together with the adjustable hand and shoulder straps, ensure your hands and fingers are always comfortable. This reduces any strain and means you can focus more fully on the music you’re playing.

Hidersine Inizio Violin

Hidersine violins are perfectly suited for student violin players. These high quality, low cost instruments cater perfectly for the needs of beginner musicians, redefining all expectations of student violins and offering the best possible start. 

The Inizio violin comes with an octagonal bow and shockproof case with detachable straps. Hidersine's 'Ready to Play' promise means you can buy this instrument with the confidence that you have everything you need to start practicing straight away.

Features of the Hidersine Inizio Violin

  • Laminated Spruce table
  • Graphic Flamed Maple back and Ribs
  • Black Hardwood Fittings
  • Ebony pegs and fingerboard
  • Seasoned maple bridge
  • Alloy tailpiece with 4 fine tuners.
  • Free shockproof violin case with detachable straps
  • Free octagonal Brazilwood stick bow

Top Quality at an Affordable Price

If you're looking for a quality student violin for sale at an accessible price, then this is the violin for you.

Hidersine Inizio instruments feature a solid Spruce top, and solid Maple ribs and backs too. These solid tonewood sections enhance the instrument’s natural tone, creating a warm, sweet tone. 

The solid Maple body is further enhanced by a ‘graphic flame’ finish - a special finishing technique which gives the appearance of top quality flamed maple, at a fraction of the cost. So your Inizio violin has the appearance of a much more exclusive instrument.

Quality Tone

The Hidersine Inizio Violin produces a tone that will continually improve as you play your new instrument. As the wood ages, and the solid tonewood relaxes, your violin will resonate even more naturally and the sound will improve further.

Built to Last

The Hidersine Inizio Violin has been built with durability in mind, making it a reliable long lasting investment.

It benefits from an ebony fingerboard and pegs. This prized hardwood is the top material of choice due to its durability. Ebony does not wear out from playing, making it sure to last. It’s also smooth on the fingers, helping make the playing experience more pleasant all round.

Choosing The Right Size

The Hidersine Vivente comes in three different sizes: 4/4, ¾, ½

Most adults and children over the age of twelve play with a full size (4/4) violin. Smaller sizes are generally used by children who can’t yet stretch down the fingerboard.

It’s important to note that it’s far better for a child to stay on a smaller instrument for a longer period of time than to buy a bigger violin and wait for them to grow into it. Playing too large an instrument can lead to discomfort and potential injuries. Buying the correct size violin will help you to avoid this situation.

If you need help to determine which size violin is right for you, then check out my handy Violin Sizing Guide. It will show you the important measurements you need to know to choose the right size violin or fiddle for you.

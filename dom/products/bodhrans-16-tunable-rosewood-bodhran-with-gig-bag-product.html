Beschreibung

 

16" Tunable Rosewood Bodhrán with Carrier Bag 

Paraic McNeela has been making professional Irish bodhráns for nearly 40 years, it's not a hobby for him, it's a passion. If you're looking to buy a bodhrán you've came to the right place! Paraic has developed this beautiful Rosewood16" Tuneable Bodhrán, to produce a rich, deep tone. 

Key Features of the 16" Tuneable Bodhrán: 

  • Produces a deep, rich yet soft tone. 
  • Stunning Rosewood rim and t-bar
  • Arm cut-out for even more playing comfort.
  • Features McNeela's 6-point trademark tuning system with Allen key (included).
  • Premium goat skin, sustainably sourced. 
  • 4.5" Rim depth.
  • Taped around the edges for extended harmonic range.
  • Comes with a free hardwood bulbous beater and lightweight gig bag.
  • *Plus BONUS LESSONS*

BONUS LESSONS WITH RÓNÁN Ó SNODAIGH

What’s included?

For a limited time only, every one of these bodhráns comes with access to eight free expertly taught bodhrán lessons by Rónán Ó Snodaigh, one of the worlds best bodhrán players and frontman for the incredible Irish trad-fusion band ‘Kíla’.

These lessons are exclusive to McNeela products and instruments and are sure to get you up and playing as soon as possible.

These lessons are exclusive to McNeela Instruments and will have you accompanying traditional Irish music sets with gusto in no time!

The Bodhrán

This isn't your average 16" tuneable bodhrán, it produces a rich, deep tone and is non-intrusive when playing in sessions and really helps bring any session together. This bodhran is perfect for a professional or beginner bodhran player as it caters to every need.  

It has a handcrafted four and a half inch rim. The rim has an added layer of wood which provides extra strength, especially while tuning. As it has a t-bar and a cut-out, it's more comfortable to play than others. The t-bar is also removable if preferred. 

Tuning System

This bodhrán features McNeela's trademark tuning system, this system allows the bodhrán reach its full potential and helps produce an unbeatable tone control and flexible volume, making it the perfect bodhrán to play in any and every situation. 

Smart Finish

With its beautifully finished rosewood frame and t-bar and carefully treated goat skin, this is a bodhrán made for life. It also comes taped around the edges as standard for a deeper, richer tone. 

Comes with a free hardwood bulbous beater and gig bag plus online lessons with Paraic!  

Goat Skin: 

The skin is the most important part of the bodhrán. That's why we only use the best goat skins. Our skins are sustainably sourced from healthy animals, carefully selected and examined for quality. They produce great depth of sound and are easy to tune.  We've done many blind tests and the results are staggering. Even non-bodhrán players can tell the difference between a good quality skin and a poor one - it's that obvious. Our skins are naturally hand-cured in lime, which gives that all-important bass tone, then 'pickled' to close up the pores and finally stretched and left to sun dry, achieving a lovely clean 'bleached' finish. 

“Whatever way Paraic cures them and treats them, I can get everything I want out of them. There’s a lovely sound to the [McNeela goatskin].” Jim Woods

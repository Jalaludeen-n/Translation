Beschreibung

Experience the Heartbeat of Irish Music

“Such a fantastic drum, perfect for a beginner.” - Gavin D.

Ready to dive deeper into Irish rhythm? The 16" Non-Tuneable Bodhrán is the drum musicians trust to deliver substantial tone with ease. Slightly larger than our 14” model, it strikes the perfect balance of offering richer bass and fuller resonance without the bulk of traditional 18” drums.

Paraic McNeela has been making bodhráns since 1979, it's not a hobby for him, it's a passion. This professionally crafted bodhrán is one of a line of drums that has been loved by beginners to professionals since then.

Crafted for comfort and ease, its balanced hardwood frame and single crossbar sit comfortably in your hands, providing confident control from your first tune. The fixed-tension goatskin head removes any complexity, letting you enjoy consistent, rich sound from the very first beat.

Each drum is made with sustainably sourced, hand-cured goatskin, expertly stretched and carefully edge-taped to deepen the bass and control overtones. You’ll instantly notice the warm, resonant tone that’s perfectly suited for both practice at home and lively pub sessions.

No gimmicks or shortcuts, just genuine craftsmanship and the authentic Irish sound you’re after.

Why is 16” the Session Player’s Favourite?

The 16” bodhrán is the preferred size among modern traditional players because it blends powerful tone with manageable handling.

Ideal for adults, it offers enough surface area for expressive playing and dynamic sound projection without the cumbersome feel of larger drums. It's a standard, session-tested size and loved around the world.

“So far superior to any bodhrán I have ever played, it's really amazing. The tonal range I can produce on it is astonishing, and the beater provided is excellent as well.” - Darius K.

Want to Avoid Buying a “Toy” Instead of an Instrument?

We understand that worry, with a market full of drums more suited for display than a good tune! That's why we built our 16” bodhrán for players who want real, satisfying sound from day one. Featuring genuine hand-cured goatskin for a deep bass and rich sound, this drum is expertly edge-taped to refine tone and eliminate unwanted ringing. It's a bodhrán you'll proudly carry into any session or jam.

Is Comfort Important When Learning?

Definitely. Our crossbar design and rim shape ensure comfortable handling, perfect for beginners and advancing enthusiasts. It sits naturally and securely in your hands, providing balance and reducing strain, so you can practice longer and develop your playing skills without fatigue or frustration.

Whether you're exploring your roots or pursuing your passion, this drum connects you authentically to tradition.

Who is the founder of McNeela Music?

McNeela Music was founded by Paraic McNeela, an Irish musician and enthusiast with a passion for providing high-quality musical instruments and accessories.

What modes of payments does McNeela Music accept?

McNeela Music offers a secure risk free payment experience. All Credit and Debit cards are accepted. Additionally, Paypal is also accepted at McNeela Music.

Does McNeela Music ship to my location?

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

When will my product be shipped?

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed. If you place an order on a weekend, it will be shipped on the next working day. Our goal is to ensure prompt delivery and get your musical instruments and accessories to you as quickly as possible. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on the destination and the shipping method selected during checkout.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email from McNeelaMusic. This email will contain a tracking number and instructions on how to track your package. If you cannot locate the email, please check your spam or junk folder as it may have been filtered there. If you still cannot find the tracking number, please reach out to our customer support at by Clicking Here.

What is the return policy at McNeela Music?

At McNeela Music, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality musical instruments and accessories. We offer a 30-day “Try-At-Home” return policy. We take great care in ensuring that our products are in excellent condition and carefully packed before being shipped.

Are the products at McNeela Music suitable for beginners?

Yes, McNeela Music caters to musicians of all skill levels, including beginners. They offer instruments and resources specifically designed to support newcomers on their musical journey.

How can I contact McNeela Music for further assistance?

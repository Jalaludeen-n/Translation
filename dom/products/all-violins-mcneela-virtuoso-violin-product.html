Beschreibung

A Violin Worth Growing Into

"I love this violin! It has a beautiful sound." - Irena K.

The McNeela Virtuoso Violin is built for the player who’s ready to step into something more. More resonance. More precision. More confidence in every note.

Crafted for advancing adult musicians, this is the violin that meets you at your current level and takes you further.

Whether you’re returning to your musical journey or refining your tone in sessions and lessons, the Virtuoso is your next step forward.

What makes it so special?

It’s based on the iconic ‘Messiah’ Stradivarius model of 1716, brought to life with 20+ year aged spruce and maple, a bespoke French-made bridge, and a finish that looks as beautiful as it sounds. Every detail is chosen to help you play with more ease, depth, and expression.

Bonus: The Virtuoso comes ready to play, complete with a responsive carbon fibre bow, premium rosin, and a velvet-lined wooden case, all free.

Product highlights

Inspired by a Legacy

Modeled after the Stradivarius ‘Messiah’ violin, this instrument channels one of the most famous violin designs in history, with sweet arching and ideal proportions for expressive playing.

You’ll get a sound you love, whether you’re playing a soft slow air or an energetic trad reel. This violin works with you and delivers a sound that encourages you to keep exploring.

The Secret to the Sound

Aged European spruce and maple bring warmth, clarity, and depth to your tone, and the French-made Despiau bridge ensures clean articulation and responsive resonance across all registers. It’s a gorgeous instrument that offers a luxury experience at a fraction of the cost.

Tone That Turns Heads

Infeld Blue strings are known for their warmth, clarity, and balanced projection, and they’re the perfect fit for the Virtuoso. These strings work beautifully with the aged tonewoods and Despiau bridge, giving you a voice that carries and connects, no matter the room or tune.

Precision Bridge for Powerful Sound

At the heart of the Virtuoso is its Despiau Chevalets bridge, handcrafted in France from the finest maple and treated with natural materials to preserve tonal integrity. This bridge amplifies clarity, sustain, and responsiveness while connecting your strings directly to the soul of the instrument..

Designed for Growth

This violin was crafted with advancing adult players in mind, with just the right blend of playability and complexity. The setup is responsive and forgiving, making the Virtuoso a pleasure to return to every day as you build skill and confidence.

Built to Last, Ready to Play

From the oil varnished finish to the carefully set bridge, every Virtuoso violin is fully inspected and play-tested before shipping. The included 65g round carbon fibre bow offers rapid response and controlled handling, perfect for ornamentation, speed, and expression.

You’ll feel the quality as soon as it’s in your hands.

What’s Included?

No need to piece everything together. With your Virtuoso violin, you’ll receive a premium velvet-lined wooden case, a professional carbon fibre bow, Hidersine 3V rosin, and a full in-house setup.

Plus, it’s fully inspected and set up in our workshop before it’s shipped out to you. Everything is handled, so you can focus on making music.

McNeela Virtuoso Violin
I immediately brought this violin to our local luthier after receiving it, given the problems we experienced with another McNeela Music violin. The luthier first pointed out that the bridge is adhered to the varnish, possibly caused by tension of the strings when set or heat exposure during shipping, Additionally, he identified spots where the varnish is absent around the edges and on the back. Can't get an accurate sound until we can tune it and that requires the bridge in correct position, so into the shop it will go in September. Due to the bridge problem and lack of varnish in areas and shop repairs I took 2 stars off. I honestly hate to give bad reviews to but felt my mother really tried to work with this company before and even gave them additional chances by trusting and purchasing more violins. Be sure to really have your violins checked.
Reply:
Hi Brandy, Thank you for the candid feedback. One point however, the other violin was a Bravura violin. As with that violin it does seem heat exposure and environmental stress, particularly heat/dryness during transit or local climate swings on it's way to Montana, has stuck the bridge to the varnish. On the points you raised about the finish touch-ups, it's hard to imagine spots with zero varnish as it's given multiple light coats, but it is finished with a distressed style to match the antique violin it's modelled off, but having said that, based on your feedback we've added another a layer of checks for both the varnish and the bridge and we have made packing/handling updates aimed at limiting heat/humidity stress in transit. And I’ve reached out by email with a goodwill gesture and warranty extension, and we will keep prioritising your family’s instruments into the future. Kind regards, Emma

Who is the founder of McNeela Music?

McNeela Music was founded by Paraic McNeela, an Irish musician and enthusiast with a passion for providing high-quality musical instruments and accessories.

What modes of payments does McNeela Music accept?

McNeela Music offers a secure risk free payment experience. All Credit and Debit cards are accepted. Additionally, Paypal is also accepted at McNeela Music.

Does McNeela Music ship to my location?

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

When will my product be shipped?

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed. If you place an order on a weekend, it will be shipped on the next working day. Our goal is to ensure prompt delivery and get your musical instruments and accessories to you as quickly as possible. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on the destination and the shipping method selected during checkout.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

How can I track my order from McNeela Music?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email from McNeelaMusic. This email will contain a tracking number and instructions on how to track your package. If you cannot locate the email, please check your spam or junk folder as it may have been filtered there. If you still cannot find the tracking number, please reach out to our customer support at by Clicking Here.

What is the return policy at McNeela Music?

At McNeela Music, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality musical instruments and accessories. We offer a 30-day “Try-At-Home” return policy. We take great care in ensuring that our products are in excellent condition and carefully packed before being shipped.

Are the products at McNeela Music suitable for beginners?

Yes, McNeela Music caters to musicians of all skill levels, including beginners. They offer instruments and resources specifically designed to support newcomers on their musical journey.

How can I contact McNeela Music for further assistance?

For any further assistance, you can reach out to McNeela Music's customer support team through their website's contact page or by emailing us at service@mcneelamusic.com

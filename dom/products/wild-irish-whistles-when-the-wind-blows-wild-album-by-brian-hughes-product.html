Product highlights

Part 1: Making The Album

This video gives a rare glimpse into the work and life of a legend in traditional Irish music, revealing how childhood memories and tradition come together as he creates an album I know I’m excited to hear.

"No musical gymnastics just really beautiful music. Loved the album" - Mossy G.

"CD arriived in the post today, super job, well done" - Eamonn Croke.

"I absolutely love Brian Hughes' new album.... Beautiful tunes, superbly played and arranged. Relaxed, musical, inspirational. I want to learn every tune! Well done Brian and all involved" - Angela K.

Part 2 - A New Take on Traditions

Recorded on the stunning Flaggy Shore at Garry Ó Briain’s renowned Creg na Vagabones studio in County Clare, this album showcases Brian’s mastery of the whistle and uilleann pipes, alongside the extraordinary talents of featured guest musicians.

"Reminds me of some classic whistle tracks from the 80’s and 90’s.. This is legend material!"  - Ketil L.

"Brilliant and fabulous" - Angela D.

"Wow!! Love Brian's playing!! And the other musicians!" - Nancy L.

Part 3 - Featured Musicians

Brian is joined on this album by some of Ireland’s finest trad musicians. Together, they create a seamless fusion of instruments, blending the whistle with the rich tones of harp, concertina, fiddle, bodhrán, guitar, mandocello, piano, scottish small pipes, and double bass to produce a truly unique sound. See what some of them have to say on this video.

"The arrangements were subtle, colorful, provacative, evocative, and flawlessly executed... I just cannot express enough how elevated I felt when I finished that last track." - Jim A.

"The mighty playing of Brian Hughes along with a host of great musicians... It's a great listen!" - Ruadhrai O.

"Your album is awesome!" - Sharon S.


Part 4 - In The Studio

Part 4 of the documentary is a fascinating behind-the-scenes look at the energy and collaboration that make traditional Irish music so special. In this episode we're joined by some incredible musicians: One of the best concertina players around, an All-Ireland-winning bodhrán champion, and a very gifted guitarist. This is something special to watch.

"I just downloaded and played the album. Wow! What a terrific experience! Excellent choice of tunes, brilliant collaborations, skillful technical production work .. the accolades will pour in." - Jim A.

"Absolutely mighty" - Ian K.

A New Collection of Classic Irish Tunes

Brian Hughes, one of Ireland’s best loved tin whistle players, is joined by some of Ireland’s finest trad musicians who accompany him on guitar, bodhrán, fiddle, the concertina and harp. The result is this collection of timeless classics, reimagined for the 21st century.

  • 15 original recordings
  • Classic tin whistle tunes
  • Jigs, reels, polkas and slow airs
  • Specially recorded in the West of Ireland

Who is the founder of McNeela Music?

McNeela Music was founded by Paraic McNeela, an Irish musician and enthusiast with a passion for providing high-quality musical instruments and accessories.

What modes of payments does McNeela Music accept?

McNeela Music offers a secure risk free payment experience. All Credit and Debit cards are accepted. Additionally, Paypal is also accepted at McNeela Music.

Does McNeela Music ship to my location?

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

When will my product be shipped?

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed. If you place an order on a weekend, it will be shipped on the next working day. Our goal is to ensure prompt delivery and get your musical instruments and accessories to you as quickly as possible. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on the destination and the shipping method selected during checkout.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

How can I track my order from McNeela Music?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email from McNeelaMusic. This email will contain a tracking number and instructions on how to track your package. If you cannot locate the email, please check your spam or junk folder as it may have been filtered there. If you still cannot find the tracking number, please reach out to our customer support at by Clicking Here.

What is the return policy at McNeela Music?

At McNeela Music, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality musical instruments and accessories. We offer a 30-day “Try-At-Home” return policy. We take great care in ensuring that our products are in excellent condition and carefully packed before being shipped.

Are the products at McNeela Music suitable for beginners?

Yes, McNeela Music caters to musicians of all skill levels, including beginners. They offer instruments and resources specifically designed to support newcomers on their musical journey.

How can I contact McNeela Music for further assistance?

For any further assistance, you can reach out to McNeela Music's customer support team through their website's contact page or by emailing us at service@mcneelamusic.com

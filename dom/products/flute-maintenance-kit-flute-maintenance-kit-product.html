McNeela Flute Maintenance Kit 

An essential kit for the maintenance of wooden flutes and whistles.

The McNeela Flute Maintenance Kit contains:

  • Oiling Rod
  • Drying Rod
  • Cork Grease
  • Grapeseed Bore Oil
  • Instruction leaflet

This pack contains a bottle of grapeseed bore oil for oiling the flute, two rods the same size; one for oiling the inner bore of the flute and the other for drying, along with cork grease plus instructions on how to maintain your flute. This is an essential pack for all owners of wooden flutes or wooden whistles to prevent the wood from drying out and splitting and improve smoothness of play and quality of tone.

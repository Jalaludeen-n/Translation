The Anglo Concertina Absolute Beginners Book
by Chris Sherburn & Dave Mallinson 

This book is for beginners of all C/G concertinas. It has all the fingering charts and notes for its impressive collection of tunes and is a great tutorial for the absolute beginner concertina player.

It's also very handy for students who don't yet read music. Full of great tips and advice, you'll find your playing will come on in leaps and bounds.

Key features of the The Anglo Concertina Absolute Beginners Book

  • Easy to follow instructions for all ages
  • Perfect for the absolute beginner
  • Suitable for those who don't read music
  • Extensive collection of tunes
  • CD accompaniment available (sold separately)

N.B. This book is for the Anglo concertina and not Duet or English concertinas.

Product Description

McNeela EasyString Student Violin

One of the best-selling starter fiddles in our Irish fiddle store, the McNeela EasyString Student Violin has been designed specially for small hands and little fingers and is perfect for young beginner players just starting to learn the violin:

Is the McNeela EasyString Student Violin right for you?

  • Available in ¼, ½, ¾ or 4/4 (full) size
  • Designed for young beginners
  • Soft, pliable strings
  • Easy to play
  • Good volume
  • Bright, clear tone
  • Compact and lightweight
  • Excellent value

The McNeela EasyString Student Violin is a perfect starter instrument, providing the perfect introduction to the violin or Irish fiddle.

Featuring pliable strings that are easy to press, this beginner violin is perfect for the young musician in your life. It would make a wonderful musical gift for a creative child.

Featuring a classic high gloss finish, the McNeela EasyString Student Violin produces a good quality tone and great volume. It also comes with a quality beginner violin bow (rosin included).

Features of the McNeela EasyString Student Violin

  • Easy play beginner strings
  • Chin rest
  • Boxwood pegs
  • Includes violin bow and rosin
  • Comes with free lightweight violin case

Irish Fiddle for Young Learners

The McNeela EasyString Student Violin is the perfect starter instrument for any beginner, especially those looking to try out an instrument for the first time.

The McNeela EasyString Student Violin is carefully crafted with ease of play for small hands in mind, enabling the young musician to take control and play with confidence, right from the start.

Easy to Play

These pliable strings are perfect for young hands, offering a great response, making them easy to press and therefore easy to play.

Note: New strings may require a little breaking in, but once you have played them for a while, the sound will continue to improve.

Keep It Safe

This lightweight violin comes with a free case to protect your new instrument from any unwanted knocks and bumps. Our high quality cases make it easier to transport your violin and will help to keep it looking like new for longer.

The violin and bow are fitted in a comfortable and extremely lightweight violin case, designed with young shoulders in mind. The case also features a roomy exterior pocket to store accessories. The compact interior is lined with emerald green velvet felt and features a separate compartment for rosin and chamois cloth, and violin bow.

The McNeela Guarantee

We provide a guarantee for any material defects or dissatisfaction. 

We proudly offer a one year guarantee on all new McNeela violins for sale, against any material defects arising from the product. Beyond one year, we will service your violin for a nominal fee. 

Money Back Trial Period

Additionally, if you're unsatisfied for any reason whatsoever, we offer a full unconditional money-back guarantee for 14 days from the day you receive your McNeela Violin.

Who is the founder of McNeela Music?

McNeela Music was founded by Paraic McNeela, an Irish musician and enthusiast with a passion for providing high-quality musical instruments and accessories.

What modes of payments does McNeela Music accept?

McNeela Music offers a secure risk free payment experience. All Credit and Debit cards are accepted. Additionally, Paypal is also accepted at McNeela Music.

Does McNeela Music ship to my location?

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

When will my product be shipped?

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed. If you place an order on a weekend, it will be shipped on the next working day. Our goal is to ensure prompt delivery and get your musical instruments and accessories to you as quickly as possible. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on the destination and the shipping method selected during checkout.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

How can I track my order from McNeela Music?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email from McNeelaMusic. This email will contain a tracking number and instructions on how to track your package. If you cannot locate the email, please check your spam or junk folder as it may have been filtered there. If you still cannot find the tracking number, please reach out to our customer support at by Clicking Here.

What is the return policy at McNeela Music?

At McNeela Music, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality musical instruments and accessories. We offer a 30-day “Try-At-Home” return policy. We take great care in ensuring that our products are in excellent condition and carefully packed before being shipped.

Are the products at McNeela Music suitable for beginners?

Yes, McNeela Music caters to musicians of all skill levels, including beginners. They offer instruments and resources specifically designed to support newcomers on their musical journey.

How can I contact McNeela Music for further assistance?

For any further assistance, you can reach out to McNeela Music's customer support team through their website's contact page or by emailing us at service@mcneelamusic.com

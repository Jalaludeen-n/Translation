Beschreibung

 The Celt 17-Fret Irish Tenor Banjo

“This is a beautiful instrument with a beautiful sound.” - Greg W.

The Celt Banjo isn’t just popular, it’s our most-loved banjo among Irish traditional music students and teachers alike. It’s been upgraded for an even better tone, feel, and longevity, so whether you're just starting your journey or returning to trad after years away, this banjo is built to grow with you.

We’ve given the Celt a solid wooden rim, enhancing resonance and warmth while also adding visual elegance. Planetary tuners make mid-session tuning smooth and reliable, while our carefully adjusted setup gives it an ease of play that’s perfect for reels, triplets, and ornaments.

And the17-fret neck gives easy playability while keeping that authentic Irish sound, so you’ll enjoy increased projection, punch, and a rich, unmistakable low G that holds its own in any session.

Product Highlights

Session-Ready Sound

With its 24-bracket closed-back design, the Celt delivers that punchy, responsive tone Irish trad players rely on. The upgraded wooden rim gives it warm projection, helping it stand out without ever sounding harsh. This is tone you can trust in a crowded room.

“My new Celt banjo is exactly what our session group needed. Its warm tone, great volume, and solid feel help to make this new addition stand out among our other trad instruments.” - Keith B.

No More Tuning Frustration

We’ve fitted the Celt with professional-grade planetary tuners, meaning a 4:1 gear ratio that allows precise tuning adjustments without slipping. Whether you’re at home, in class, or halfway through a pub session, you’ll stay in tune and ready to play.

“Nicely made, lots of mahogany, a top-line Remo head, well-clamped on. Has that traditional banjo sound and stays in tune very well.” - Tom S.

Trad-Optimised Playability

The 17-fret neck, GDAE tuning, and hand-selected D’Addario strings give the Celt a feel that’s responsive and comfortable. The action is adjusted by our team of trad musicians, so it’s ready to play, right out of the box.

Built to Last and Grow with You

Whether this is your first step into trad or an upgrade from a starter banjo, the Celt is built to support years of playing. From its solid mahogany construction to its stable hardware and clear tone, it’s designed to evolve with your skill.

Irish Craftsmanship You Can See

With its Celtic motif design and eye-catching gloss mahogany finish, the Celt looks like it belongs on stage or in a crowded session. It’s built with care, not mass-produced, and you can see and feel the difference.

Ready for Any Playing Environment

The Celt’s 24-bracket, closed-back design keeps the head tension even and the tone consistent, no matter the room or weather. It’s a banjo that works just as well in a pub session as it does at home or in a lesson.

Musician Setup Makes All the Difference

Each Celt Banjo is professionally set up by our workshop team before it leaves McNeela. That means smooth action, quality you can count on, and a banjo that plays clean right out of the box. No buzzing or guesswork. Just great music.

Who is the founder of McNeela Music?

McNeela Music was founded by Paraic McNeela, an Irish musician and enthusiast with a passion for providing high-quality musical instruments and accessories.

What modes of payments does McNeela Music accept?

McNeela Music offers a secure risk free payment experience. All Credit and Debit cards are accepted. Additionally, Paypal is also accepted at McNeela Music.

Does McNeela Music ship to my location?

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

When will my product be shipped?

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed. If you place an order on a weekend, it will be shipped on the next working day. Our goal is to ensure prompt delivery and get your musical instruments and accessories to you as quickly as possible. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on the destination and the shipping method selected during checkout.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

How can I track my order from McNeela Music?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email from McNeelaMusic. This email will contain a tracking number and instructions on how to track your package. If you cannot locate the email, please check your spam or junk folder as it may have been filtered there. If you still cannot find the tracking number, please reach out to our customer support at by Clicking Here.

What is the return policy at McNeela Music?

At McNeela Music, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality musical instruments and accessories. We offer a 30-day “Try-At-Home” return policy. We take great care in ensuring that our products are in excellent condition and carefully packed before being shipped.

Are the products at McNeela Music suitable for beginners?

Yes, McNeela Music caters to musicians of all skill levels, including beginners. They offer instruments and resources specifically designed to support newcomers on their musical journey.

How can I contact McNeela Music for further assistance?

For any further assistance, you can reach out to McNeela Music's customer support team through their website's contact page or by emailing us at service@mcneelamusic.com

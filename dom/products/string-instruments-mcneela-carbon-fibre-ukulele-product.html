Beschreibung

McNeela Moonshine Ukulele
 
The ukulele is a perfect introductory instrument for musicians of any age or ability and the McNeela Moonshine Ukulele is no exception. 
 
This 15 fret soprano ukulele is built from carbon fibre, making it an incredibly durable and robust, long lasting instrument.

This is a compact, lightweight portable but sturdy instrument that's ideal for travel and would make the perfect gift for the music lover in your life.

Features of the McNeela Ukulele:

  • Soprano ukulele
  • 15 fret
  • 4 string
  • Carbon fibre body
  • Sealed gear machine head
  • Available in black 

The Advantages of Carbon Fibre

Carbon fibre is a robust polymer that's five times stronger than steel. It provides a more robust and durable alternative to wood. Carbon fibre also has the added benefit of not warping like organic materials. It's also largely unaffected by changes in temperature or humidity making it ideal for those who live in more extreme climates.

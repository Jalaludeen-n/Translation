Beschreibung

McNeela Carbon 30 Violin Case (Oblong Shape)

Easy-going protection you can count on

 

This oblong case blends 30% carbon fibre with polycarbonate for a shell that is lighter than pure plastic, yet just as tough. Loading in and out of rehearsals feels effortless, while your violin stays safe from bumps.

 

Inside, a floating suspension cradle lifts the violin clear of the walls for added protection. Four bow holders line the lid, a full-width pocket keeps sheet music flat, and a deep bin stores rosin, shoulder rest, and spare strings for easy access, so your instrument is safe from rattles and scratches when you’re on the go.

 

Lightweight and ready for the road

 

The case weighs about 1.9 kg and rides easily on padded backpack straps. A tongue and groove seal keeps out rain, dust, and sudden temperature swings, so you arrive ready to dive in and enjoy the music.

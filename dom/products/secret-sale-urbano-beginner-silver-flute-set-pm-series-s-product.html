McNeela “Urbano” Beginner Concert Flute Set [PM Series]

"This concert flute set will get you started in style."

The McNeela Urbano Silver Boehm Concert Flute (PM Series) serves all types of music beautifully, including classical and folk music.

With a Split E mechanism, Offset G key, closed holes, and silver-plated nickel headjoint, body, and keys, The Urbano is a great beginner’s flute, suitable for adults and children from the age of 10yrs and over.

It has a bright, clear tone and makes it easy to get started and build confidence.

It also comes with everything you need, right out of the box: a protective hard case, a soft leather carry case, a flute maintenance kit, and McNeela’s 30-day money-back guarantee.

What modes of payments does McNeela Music accept?

McNeela Music offers a secure risk free payment experience. All Credit and Debit cards are accepted. Additionally, Paypal is also accepted at McNeela Music.

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

When will my product be shipped?

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed. Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

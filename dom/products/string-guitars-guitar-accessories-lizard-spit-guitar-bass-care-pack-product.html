Lizard Spit Guitar & Bass Care Pack

Lizard Spit Guitar and Bass Care Pack includes a 4oz bottle of guitar polish, a 4oz bottle of fretboard conditioner and a micro fibre polish cloth - everything you need to keep your instrument in the best possible condition!

  • Lizard Spit Guitar Polish: A highly effective, tried and tested, guitar polish made from carnauba wax and water. Eco-friendly, non-toxic and non-hazardous, Lizard Spit is the safest polish on the market.
  • Lizard Spit Fret Board Conditioner: A superb high quality fret board conditioner that will leave your instrument refreshed, keeping it looking and sounding great.
  • Lizard Spit Super-Fine Micro Fibre Cloth: 12" x 12" and washable.

Lizard Spit are committed to producing eco-friendly, non-toxic, non-hazardous, biodegradable and safe products so that musicians can leave a lasting impression for all the right reasons.

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed.

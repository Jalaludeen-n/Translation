Beschreibung

Brunswick BT200 Acoustic Travel Guitar

Built with a shorter scale length than a regular acoustic, the BT200 is a compact, lightweight, 3/4 size travel guitar that's perfect for young musicians and traveling musicians alike. Whether you're looking for your first guitar, or an instrument that's easily transportable for festivals, roadtrips or even travel abroad, this quality guitar offers an enjoyable playing experience for all.

Light on the fingers, and wonderfully playable, the BT200 manages to pack a punch, offering good volume despite its smaller size. Its mahogany satin-finished body produces a rich, mellow, resonant tone that's perfect for playing any style of music.

To learn more about this delightful guitar, check out the video below from Brunswick, exploring the full BT200 acoustic guitar range:

 Features of The Brunswick BT200 Travel Guitar: 

  • Sapele Mahogany Top, Back and Sides
  • Enclosed Die-Cast Machineheads
  • Satin Finish

Bonus Features:

  • Gigbag Included

The Brunswick BT200 comes with a free guitar gig bag, for easy transport and to help keep your guitar looking and sounding its best. With nylon weaving, this is a strong gig bag that's comfortable and easy to carry, using either the carry handle or double back straps. There's also a pocket on the front for extra storage.

About The Maker

Established in London in 1995, Brunswick produce British-designed acoustic guitars and basses. Like McNeela Instruments, Brunswick's goal is to offer high quality instruments at affordable prices, offering exceptional value for money. Brunswick Guitars are suitable for players of all ages and levels, from young musicians and beginners all the way through to professional performing musicians. With over 25 years’ experience producing quality instruments that are not only easy to play but sound good too, Brunswick guitars are the go-to choice for thousands of guitar players around the world.

Yes, McNeela Music ships worldwide, allowing musicians from different countries to enjoy their products and services. McNeela Music offers fast shipping to the United States, United Kingdom, and Rest of the world at a reduced rate, ensuring prompt delivery to customers around the globe.

When will my product be shipped?

McNeela Music strives to provide efficient order processing and shipping. In most cases, orders are shipped the same day or the next day after they are placed. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on the destination and the shipping method selected during checkout.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

How can I track my order from McNeela Music?

If you cannot locate the email, please check your spam or junk folder as it may have been filtered there. If you still cannot find the tracking number, please reach out to our customer support at by Clicking Here.

What is the return policy at McNeela Music?

At McNeela Music, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality musical instruments and accessories. We offer a 30-day “Try-At-Home” return policy. We take great care in ensuring that our products are in excellent condition and carefully packed before being shipped.

Are the products at McNeela Music suitable for beginners?

Yes, McNeela Music caters to musicians of all skill levels, including beginners. They offer instruments and resources specifically designed to support newcomers on their musical journey.

How can I contact McNeela Music for further assistance?

For any further assistance, you can reach out to McNeela Music's customer support team through their website's contact page or by emailing us at service@mcneelamusic.com

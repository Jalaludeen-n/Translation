Irish Flute Buyer's Guide

Why quality counts:

“A beginner flute should be a good and capable instrument in its own right. Anything less and the beginner will give up.” - TheSession.org

We know that students who use low-quality instruments will be more likely to stop practising, or even worse, quit altogether. Thats why we only provide the highest standard of instruments, as we know lesser quality instruments would be doing a disservice to you, and may hinder you from achieving your goals.

Why we use Rosewood

We only use the best quality tonal wood in all our instruments. Rosewood is a hardwood and is quite dense and heavy. The more dense the wood the better the resonance. Rosewood is hard enough to allow fora strong earthy resonance and be cost-effective at the same - exactly what we want. CITES exemption applies to all musical instruments crafted from Rosewood. For more information visit: CITES.org

How we make our Flutes

We use a tried and tested sealing technique which ensures that the flute doesn't leak precious air and enables you to produce that all-important strong low D. The Cygnet Rosewood flute has been crafted with a slightly smaller aperture which allows easy, unstressed air control and helps students learn the proper breathing patterns, and also provides more control over both the smoothness and crispness of the sound.

Flute Tuning

Our careful and precise tuning process during the manufacturing stage means you can play each note in tune with confidence and ease leaving you to focus on your performance. Our tuning slide ensures you'll always be able to adjust the pitch to adapt to any playing situation from a session environment to outdoor recitals and everything in between.

Easy to Play

The Cygnet was specifically designed for students who want to advance quickly and helps students learn the proper breathing patterns required for producing music on a wooden flute.

Visual beauty to rival its
auditory pleasure

Cygnet is finished to the highest standard. It has a beautiful naturally treated rosewood finish and to protect its good looks it comes with a free premium foam-lined hard case.

The McNeela Guarantee

We provide guarantee for any material defects or dissatisfaction. We prouduly offer a 1 year Guarantee* on the audio jack adaptor against any material defect arising from the product. We are quite comfortable with this knowing the quality that went into manufacturing process. Even beyond the one year , we will service the adaptor for a nominal cost.

Shipping costs are calculated at the checkout page based on various factors, including the destination country, package weight, and chosen shipping method. This ensures accurate and transparent pricing for customers.

What is the return policy at McNeela Music?

At McNeela Music, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality musical instruments and accessories. We offer a 30-day “Try-At-Home” return policy. We take great care in ensuring that our products are in excellent condition and carefully packed before being shipped.

Are the products at McNeela Music suitable for beginners?

Yes, McNeela Music caters to musicians of all skill levels, including beginners. They offer instruments and resources specifically designed to support newcomers on their musical journey.

Whether you’re heading to a classic trad gig with your accordion, or looking to jam outside the comfort of your home without the fear of getting your accordion damaged, McNeela Accordion Gig Bags are just for you! We have a number of premium accordion gig bags designed for storing, and transporting your accordion when you’re playing on-the-go. 

 Design made to last. 
 
All of our

accordion bags

are made from top-tier, heavy duty polyester with a soft padding on the interior and rubber floor protectors to ensure that your accordion is kept safe and snug. Each accordion gig bag is zip locked and also uses a set of exterior buckles to keep the accordion secure. In order to store any other necessary pieces to accompany an accordion player, the bags also have a set of side pockets and a pocket to ensure the storage of any other nifty accordion accessories.
 
For ease of carry, each of our bags also has a top handle to make it easier. However, to make the transportation process a breeze, our very own McNeela Accordion Gig Bag With Wheels, features a set of wheels on the bottom side of the gig bag, making the product more agile in travelling with it anywhere!
 
 Our Accordion Bags are budget friendly! 

 

Not only are our gig bags made from premium materials, but are also made with affordability in mind. Unlike other well known brands such as D’luca pro, or the black Fuselli or blue Fuselli bags, McNeela accordion bags are entirely budget friendly, ensuring you won’t have to pay a tremendous price for a quality product. We also offer a plethora of accordion accessories such as instructional books and CD's to those looking to sharpen their skills!

 

Discover our range of accordions currently on offer!

 

Note: our accordion bags are suitable for button accordions (diatonic and two-row chromatic accordions) but may not fit standard piano accordions or bass accordions.

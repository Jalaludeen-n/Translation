What Makes a Good Concertina Strap?

A good strap should provide your hand with the stability it needs without restricting your movement. Our concertina hand straps are designed to hold your hands securely in place, while still allowing the flexibility needed for fast ornamentation and expressive playing.

Frequently Asked Questions About Concertina Straps

What are concertina straps for? +

Concertina straps are designed to hold your hands securely on the instrument while you play. They provide stability, improve control, and help maintain proper technique—especially important during fast playing or long sessions.

Do I need hand straps to play a concertina? +

Yes, concertina hand straps are essential for most players. They allow you to apply pressure to the bellows and keep your hands in the correct position, making it easier to play comfortably and with accuracy.

Are McNeela concertina straps adjustable? +

Absolutely. All of our concertina straps are fully adjustable, so you can customise the fit to your hand size and playing preferences. This ensures both comfort and control, whether you're at home or on stage.

What are concertina straps made from? +

Our concertina hand straps are made from high-quality, durable leather. They're designed to last and offer a comfortable fit while withstanding regular use, making them a great long-term investment for your concertina.

Can I replace the straps on my concertina? +

Yes, replacing your concertina straps is easy. Whether you're upgrading or swapping out a worn pair, our straps are compatible with most English and Anglo concertina models and can be installed in just a few steps.

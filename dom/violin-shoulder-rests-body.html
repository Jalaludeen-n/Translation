We have a range of 

violin shoulder rests

, suitable for a variety of fiddle and violin sizes.
 
Violin shoulder rests prevent you from hunching your shoulder or stretching your neck, eliminating bad habits right from the start. Shoulder rests are recommended for beginners as they help violin players to develop correct violin posture. Violin shoulder rests also relieve pressure from the collarbone, ensuring a comfortable playing experience.
 
A quality shoulder rest will promote proper head, neck, and shoulder alignment for stress-free playing and better overall comfort.

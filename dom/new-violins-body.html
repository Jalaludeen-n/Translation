At McNeela Instruments, we’re constantly looking to bring in the latest violin models to help expand our selection. All of our new violin models are top quality instruments and are catered towards all player levels. Whether it be beginners, intermediate, or advanced players, we’re sure to have something for you to help you along your violin playing journey!

Our new violin models mainly consist of our very own made violins, and violin models made by the renowned Hidersine, who have been a powerful violin manufacturer for over a century. These violins are mostly fully sized 4/4 models, but we do also stock a number of smaller half-size and ¾ size violin models to suit younger students.

Our new models however not only stock your traditional classic violin, but also some brand new electric ones which blend old age traditions with modern technology. Electric violins allow the opportunity to alter and customise your violin sound through external amplification while still being able to use traditional technique, making it the best of both worlds.

On top of selling some brand new violin models, we also provide an equally top grade carbon fibre violin bow, violin rosin, instructional books, hard cases, and many other accessories to help you hit the ground running with your new violin!

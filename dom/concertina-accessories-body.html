Frequently Asked Questions About Concertina Accessories

What accessories do I need for my concertina? +

Every player should have basic concertina accessories like a good case, straps, and cleaning tools. Depending on your needs, you may also want tuning kits, bellows supports, or spare valves for ongoing maintenance.

Are McNeela accessories compatible with all concertinas? +

Most of our accessories for concertina are designed to work with standard models of Anglo concertina and English concertina. Be sure to check the product descriptions for compatibility or reach out to us for advice.

Do your accessories fit concertinas from other brands? +

Many of our accessories are universal or adjustable, meaning they can fit concertinas from other makers as well. Our concertina straps and cases are popular options for a wide range of instruments.

How do I know which accessories are right for my concertina? +

Our team is happy to help you choose the right gear based on your instrument type and playing style. We’ve grouped all of our concertina accessories by category to make selection quick and easy.

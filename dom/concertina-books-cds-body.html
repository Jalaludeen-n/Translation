Concertina Books for All Levels

In our collection of concertina books, you'll find step-by-step tutors, traditional tune collections, technical exercises, and theory guides—ideal for self-study or as a companion to our online concertina lessons.

These informative and practical resources are the perfect way to deepen your learning and enjoy the concertina even more.

At McNeela, we offer a carefully curated selection of materials for both Anglo concertina and English concertina players. Many of our concertina books are written by expert musicians with years of experience in traditional music.

Explore Our Concertina Artist Books and Recordings

If you're looking to dive deeper into the expression and style of the instrument, our range of concertina artist books and CDs provides a unique window into the techniques and playing of some of the finest performers. These resources are an excellent way to connect with the roots of Irish traditional music and learn directly from respected artists.

Each book or CD includes authentic tunes, ornamentation tips, and interpretive insights that will enrich your practice—whether you're playing a beginner concertina or an advanced model—and inspire you to develop your own musical voice.

Frequently Asked Questions About Concertina Books and CDs

What kind of content is included in McNeela's concertina books? +

Our concertina books include a mix of exercises, traditional Irish tunes, music theory, and step-by-step guidance. They’re suitable for all levels and help you build both technique and repertoire.

Are there books available for both Anglo and English concertina? +

Yes! We offer books specifically tailored for both Anglo concertina and English concertina. Each one is designed to match the layout and playing style of your instrument.

Can I use these books without taking lessons? +

Absolutely. Our concertina books are ideal for self-study. They provide clear instructions, illustrations, and audio support (in some cases), so you can progress confidently on your own.

What are concertina artist books? +

Concertina artist books focus on the unique playing styles, tune selections, and techniques of well-known performers. They’re a great way to learn from respected traditional musicians and explore new musical ideas.

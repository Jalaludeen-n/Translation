Frequently Asked Questions About Second-Hand Concertinas

Is it worth buying a second-hand concertina? +

Yes, buying a second-hand concertina can be worth it if the instrument is in good condition. It’s often a more affordable way to access higher-quality instruments that may be out of budget when new.

How can I tell if a used concertina is in good condition? +

Check for signs of damage like worn bellows, sticking buttons, or tuning issues. Ideally, purchase from a reputable seller who services and inspects the instrument before sale.

Where can I buy a trustworthy second-hand concertina? +

Specialist music shops like McNeela Music offer carefully inspected and refurbished second-hand concertinas, often with return policies and customer support.

There are very few traditional Irish instruments as iconic or well known as the tin whistle. That’s why at McNeela Music, we’re always sure to have a wide range of some of the most renowned tin-whistle brands in traditional Irish music. We offer a selection of whistles by Clarke for all levels of musicians. Created by Robert Clarke in 1843, Clarke Tinwhistles is a brand renowned for their history and craftsmanship. And since their start, they have become a really well-known tin whistle manufacturer in the tin whistle world.


These whistles are perfect for musicians of all levels. One of our most popular; The  ‘Clarke Black Whistle’ in D, has a tapered bore that gives off a lovely warm tone (especially in the lower notes). Plus, The ‘Clarke Sweetone’ in C has a more modern sound and design with its plastic mouthpiece and durable tin body.

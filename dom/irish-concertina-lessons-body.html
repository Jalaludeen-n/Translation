Frequently Asked Questions About Concertina Lessons

Can I learn the concertina as a beginner? +

Yes, the concertina is beginner-friendly and suitable for all ages. With regular lessons and practice, most students can play simple tunes within a few weeks.

How long does it take to learn the concertina? +

Most beginners can play basic melodies after 2–3 months of lessons. Mastering traditional styles, like Irish music, usually takes consistent practice over one to two years.

Do I need my own concertina to start lessons? +

Yes. Having your own concertina is important so you can practise between lessons. Many teachers recommend starting with a beginner or second-hand model to get familiar with the instrument.

Should I choose Anglo or English concertina for lessons? +

If your goal is to play Irish traditional music, the Anglo concertina is the best option. The English concertina is more common for classical and folk accompaniment.

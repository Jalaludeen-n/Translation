Browse our range of expertly-crafted bones, made from a variety of tonewoods including ash, ebony and rosewood.

The bones, or rhythm bones, are Ireland's oldest percussive Irish folk instrument. Today this versatile Irish percussion instrument features a modern design, to facilitate comfort and ease of play and offers a variety of rhythms and sounds.

Learn to play the unique one-handed Irish bones percussion style using our specially crafted instruments.

The ‘Low Tin Whistle’ is a unique version of the modern tin whistle. They’re typically tuned an octave lower than a normal tin whistle and produce a flute-like and mellow tone that has a different feel to higher-pitched whistles. These whistles are often tuned to keys like D, C, and F as they’re some of the most common keys in Irish music.
